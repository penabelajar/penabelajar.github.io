9. Contoh dialog accepting an invitation dan declining invitation


Contoh dialog accepting an invitation dan declining invitation adalah sebagai berikut.


Accepting an invitation

A: Hey, my parents got extra tickets to attend a movie premier.

B: Cool! What movie is that?

A: It’s Bohemian Rhapsody. Have you watched it?

B: Nope, not yet. How about you?

A: Not yet. That’s why I’m planning to ask you come with me to the cinema. Are you free tonight?

B: Sounds great! And yes actually I'm free tonight.

A: Okay, then. I’ll see you today on 7PM sharp.

B: See you!


Declining an invitation

A: Hey, what are you doing on the weekend? Would you like to join me and my brothers for camping?

B: Sounds fun! I’d love to, but I can’t. I have to stay at home, it will be my parent’s 15th year of wedding anniversary. Maybe another time I will be able.

A: Wow, congratulations to them. Anyway, that’s too bad that you can’t join. Hopefully next time you’ll be free.

B: Yes hopefully!

11. buat contoh dialog "accepting and declining invitation"


Jawaban:

Rudi: Hi Rita!

Rita: Oh, Hi Rudi, you look happy today.

Rudi: Yes, I am so happy today. The reason is that I am planning on my birthday party.

Rita: Birthday party? Sounds great.

Rudi: Yeah. I'd like to invite you to come.

Rita: I would love to. When is it?

Rudi: It will be on this Saturday evening, at my place.

Rita: This Saturday evening? Oh I am sorry I can't. I have to go to Bandung to visit my grandmother. She has been ill since last week.

Rudi: Oh I see, I understand. But next month is the opening ceremony of our new restaurant. You must come. There will be free meals and drinks.

Rita: I will definitely come. Can I bring my sister?

Rudi: Of course! The more the merrier.

Penjelasan:

Dalam dialog di atas sekaligus ada penolakan dan penerimaan undangan.

Semoga bermanfaat.
