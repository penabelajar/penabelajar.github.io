How to make lemon ice tea

Materials :

1. a glass of tea

2. two spoonfuls of sugar

3. ice cubes

4. a slice of lemon tea

Steps :

1. make the tea a bit strong because you add the lemon, it will get thinner.

2. after the tea is ready, squeeze the lemon according to your own taste.

3. add some ice cubes, and your lemon tea is ready to serve. Finally, let's drink it.

Ingredients (Bahan-bahan):Tea bag (teh celup)
Ice cube (potongan es berbentuk kubus)
Sugar (gula)
Water (air)

Steps (Langkah-langkah):1) First, put some water into a glass (Pertama, masukkan air ke dalam gelas).
2) Second, remain the tea bag into the glass. Wait until the water looks brown-red. (Kedua, rendam teh celup ke dalam gelas. Tunggu hingga air berwarna merah kecoklatan).
3) After that, Put some 2 teaspoon of sugar (Ketiga, masukkan, 2 sendok teh gula).
4) Then, mix well (Keempat, Aduk hingga merata).Fifth, put 2 or 3 pieces of ice cube into the glass of tea (Kelima, masukkan 2 atau 3 potongan es ke dalam teh).
5) Finally the ice tea is ready to serve
