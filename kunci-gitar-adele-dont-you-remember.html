11. help please i dont remember​


Jawaban:

1. Main clause: My mother was cooking

Subordinate clause: My father came home

2. Main clause: She is not doing her job (itu yang di soal salah harusnya 'her' bukan 'his')

Subordinate clause: She got punished

3. Main clause: Breakfast is important

Subordinate clause: Breakfast make our body more active

4. Main clause: She gets a good score

Subordinate clause: She still studies well

5. Main clause: To make a good dough

Subordinate clause: Cover it using a napkin around

Penjelasan:

Main clause itu kalimat utama, sedangkan subordinate clause itu kalimat penjelasnya atau kalimat tambahan aja
