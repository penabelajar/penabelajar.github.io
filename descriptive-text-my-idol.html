5. Make a descriptive text about your idol in five sentences​


Descriptive text about your idol in five sentences can be seen as follows.

My idol is Taylor Swift. Her full name is Taylor Alison Swift. She is an American singer and songwriter. She is beautiful and tall. She has straight blonde hair and beautiful green eyes. She loves singing. She has a lot of good songs.

Penjelasan:

Descriptive text adalah jenis text dalam bahasa inggris yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan atau menggambarkan sesuatu atau seseorang.

Generic Structure of Descriptive Text

1. Identification, berisi pengenalan mengenai benda atau hewan atau orang yang akan dideskripsikan.

2. Description, berisi penjelasan atau gambaran mengenai objek yang dikenalkan pada bagian identification.

Language Feature

1. Menggunakan simple present tense

2. Menggunakan banyak kata sifat (adjective)

3. Menggunakan kata kerja penghubung (relating verb)

6. make a descriptive text of your idol?​


Katy Perry

Katy's bio

Katy Perry was born on October 25, 1984 in Santa Barbara, California, United States is an American singer-songwriter and actress. She is the second child of two pastors. She has an older sister and younger brother. Perrys mothers aunt and uncle were screenwriter Eleanor Perry and director Frank Perry. she was raised by Christian pastor parents, Perry grew up listening to only gospel music and sang in her local church as a child.

Katy's profile

Full name : Katheryn Elizabeth Hudson

Nick name : Katy Perry

Date of birth : October 25, 1984

Place of birth : Santa Barbara, California, United States

Genres : Pop, pop rock, dance-pop, electropop, europop, CCM

Occupations : Singer-songwriter, actress

Instruments : Vocals, guitar, piano

Katy's Career

After earning a GED during her freshman year of high school, she began to pursue a career in music. As Katy Hudson, she released a self-titled gospel album in 2001. She adopted the stage name Katy Perry and released her first Internet single Ur So Gay that November, which garnered public attention but failed to chart.She rose to fame with the release of her second single I Kissed a Girl in 2008, which went on to top international charts.

Her sophomore studio album Teenage Dream was released in August 2010 and debuted at number one on the Billboard 200. The album included hit singles California Gurls, Teenage Dream, Firework and E.T. , all of which topped the charts on the Billboard Hot 100, making the Teenage Dream album one of only nine albums to ever produce four or more Hot 100 number ones. With E.T. at number one on the chart of May 12, 2011, Perry became the first artist in history to spend a whole year, 52 consecutive weeks, in the top 10 of Billboard Hot 100.

Perry was credited as a guest judge on the seventh series of British television show The X Factor; has released a fragrance called Purr; and will appear in the upcoming 2011 film The Smurfs.

PERTANYAAN : Write a descriptive text of your idol teacher at school.

My teacher is Mrs. Shinta, she is my favorite teacher, she is someone who wants to teach her students some very useful things. Mrs. Shinta is a patriot of knowledge for us at school, she is a kind and patient person, so many students at school really like being taught by Mrs. Shinta.

Mrs. Shinta is very friendly, kind, patient and loving. His guidance really inspires us even though it's a little thing but it can be a big thing that is useful for us, namely the knowledge he teaches. Later, when we are all grown up, the knowledge that my teacher gives will be very useful for my goals, so we thank my teacher, Mrs. Shinta.

Discussion

The definition of descriptive text is a text where the main idea is in the form of a delivery by describing a particular object, place and event in more detail. Therefore, the reader of the description text will be able to seem to feel both physically and feel emotions directly with what is described in the text.

Description text features:

Describe or explain something.

A more detailed and involving explanation of the five senses.

Trying to make readers and listeners feel the scenes or events in the text.

Involves all five senses to make the reader understand.

The sentence focuses on one thing that fits the explanation.

Description text structure:

Title - The title of the essay represents a story which is an implied summary of the entire writing. This title serves as an attraction for interest and a promotional name.

Identification - the determination of the identity of people, objects and other things.

Classification - is an element of the compiler with a system of a group in accordance with the rules and standards that have been previously established.

Description - which contains pictures or contains an explanation of the object or thing to be discussed in the description text.

Conclusion - the conclusion is an affirmation on things that are considered more important. Conclusions may or may not be included.
