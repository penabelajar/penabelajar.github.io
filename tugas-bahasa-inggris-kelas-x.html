3. tolong bantu tugas bahasa inggris kelas X sma. halaman 42


Soal pada buku bahasa Inggris kelas X halaman 42 adalah melengkapi conversation atau percakapan sesuai dengan hint atau clue yang sudah diberikan.

Pembahasan  

Jawaban tugas buku bahasa Inggris kelas X halaman 42 adalah:

Riri  : Let’s do something together this long weekend.

Santi : It’s a great idea! What about going to Cinema?

Bayu : Or we could do something more interesting?

Riri   : I would like to go to Bromo Mountain with Mia and Andi for hiking.

Santi : Really? It sounds interesting. I want to join with you Riri!

Bayu : Emmmm... Sounds good, but what will you do there?

Riri  : We will go hiking and also watch sunrise from the top of Bromo mountain

Santi : Ah, I am really excited! Would you join with Riri too, Bayu?

Bayu : I would rather stay at home. I can't go hiking due to my asthma.

Riri   : I am sorry to hear that Bayu. May be we can go somewhere else someday.

Santi : I am really sorry to hear that too Bayu.

Bayu : Don't worry mate. I'll be okay staying at home. Good luck for your hiking.

Riri   : Okay, Can we go somewhere with Bayu today?

Santi : So, we are going to accompany you to go to the cinema now. Do you agree Bayu?

Bayu : Sure. You are really my friends.

a. Definition of Label

A label is a detailed description of a product that is written on the outside of the packaging, with the aim of providing information on how to use or the composition of a product.

With the label, consumers or us who will use the product are clearer about the language or how to use it.

b. Product Label Purpose

The objectives are as follows:

1. Provide detailed information about the contents of the labeled product without having to open the packaging.

2. As a means of communication between producers and consumers about things that consumers need to know about the product, especially things that are visible or not known physically.

3. Providing appropriate instructions to consumers to obtain optimum product function.

4. Advertising means for producers.

5. Provide a "sense of security" for consumers.
