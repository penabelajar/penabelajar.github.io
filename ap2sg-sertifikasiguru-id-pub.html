13. in the evenings we usually (go)...…..to a pub​


Present simple:

In the evenings, we usually go to a pub.

PEMBAHASANSimple Present Tense

Yaitu bentuk tenses yang digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang sedang berlangsung atau berulangkali.

RUMUS

Positive Sentences:

Subject + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + Object

Negative Sentences:

Subject + do/does + not + Verb 1 + ObjectSubject + to be + not + Object

Interrogative Sentences:

Do/does + Subject + Verb 1 + Object + ?To be + Subject + Object + ?

Time signals

~ Usually

~ Often

~ Everyday

~ Every minutes

~ At the moment

~ Right now

Etc.

- To be -

Is untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)

Am untuk subjek I

Are untuk subjek You/We/They dan subjek jamak

Do

Digunakan untuk subjek I/You/We/They dan subjek jamak

Does

Digunakan untuk subjek orang ketiga tunggal (He/She/It)

~ Based on Question

Change verb in the bracket!

In the evenings, we usually (go) to a pub.

Terdapat kata usually yang berarti 'biasanya' maka pasti termasuk kalimat Present Simple.

In the evenings, we usually (go) to a pub.

➡ In the evenings, we usually go to a pub.
