1. contoh 10 soal pilihan ganda tentang suggestion beserta jawabannya


1. A: What ___________ we do this weekend?
    B: Let's go to the beach.
     a. will     b. is           c. shall       d. should

2. How about _____________ surfing?
    a. go    b. going       c. do         d. doing

3. X: Let's throw a party this Friday. What do you say?
    C: Yes,___________
    a. we can          b. that would be great.
    c. thank you      d. I do.

4. ____________ we go to the cafe? It's still too early to go into the cinema now.
    a. why not       b. shall we do      c. what shall      d. why don't

5. _________ order some food? I'm starving now.
   a. Why             b. let's         c. shall we       d. by the way

6. E: How shall we go to the party?
    B: Shall we take my car?
    E: ___________
    a. That'll be great              b. I think it's nice.   
    c. I suppose you're right   d. I'll have a look at it.

7. R: What about going fishing?
    K: Sorry, ____________.
    a. I'll be busy          b. I'm not really into it.
    c. I can't play it.        d. I think it's not okay.

8. L: What time shall we meet?
    P: What ___________ two pm?
    a. on    b. of       c. is      d. about

9. Why don't we ________ a ride in the countryside?
     a. go for      b. go to      c. go in        d. go by

10. H: Shall we go camping this weekend?
       N: Sorry, man. I don't really fancy it. _______________
       a. I'd prefer going skiing
       b. I prefer to go skiing
       c. I'd love to go skiing
       d. I'd rather go skiing

jawaban: c/b/b/d/c/a/b/d/a/d

Jawaban:

Suggestion : To give a suggestion that is to introduce or purpose an idea or a plan to someone's consuderation.

Offer : To give something physical or abstract to someone, which can be taken as trade.

B : Hi, A.

A : Oh, really? Can I help you? Maybe I can find it.

B : Thaky you so much A, you are an angel.

What kind of interpersonal transaction is going on in the conversation given above?

Jawaban :

It's an offer, 'cause A offers help to B.

Jawaban:

Suggestion atau dalam bahasa Indonesianya adalah saran, yaitu sebuah kalimat yang digunakan untuk memberi saran atau nasihat kepada seseorang agar orang yang diberi saran itu mau melakukan atau tidak melakukan sesuatu sesuai dengan saran yang diiberikannya itu.

Kata kunci yang selalu digunakan untuk memberi saran agar seseorang itu melakukan sesuatu biasanya antara lain adalah: should, ought to, it better for you to verb-1.  Sedangkan saran agar seseorang tidak melakukan sesuatu adalah bentuk negatif dari itu, yaitu: shouldn't, oughtn't to, it is better for you not to verb-1.

Contoh:

1. You should learn more about English by reading various English articles.

Kamu seharusnya mempelajari lebih banyak bahasa Inggris dengan membcaca artikel bahasa Inggris yang berfariasi.

(agar melakukan sesuatu)

1. You shouldn't feel bored about that. (agar tidak melakukan sesuatu)

Kemudian kamu menjawab: Thank you for your suggestion.

Kamu seharusnya mempelajari lebih banyak bahasa Inggris dengan cara membaca artikel dalam bahasa Inggris yang berfariasi.

Offer yang artinya tawaran adalah kalimat yang digunakan untuk menawarkan seseuatu misalnya saja jasa, ataupun bantuan atau pelayanan kepada seseorang.

Contoh:

1. May help you learn English by giving some ways to master English?

Bolehkah saya membatumu mempelajari bahasa Inggris dengan cara memberi beberapa cara untuk menguasai bahada Inggris?

Yes, please. I would be very happy then.

5. 10 contoh soal dan jawaban tentang giving suggestion


13 Sept 2018.

Contoh 10 pertanyaan pilihan ganda tentang giving suggestion.

1. Tom looks very tired. What's your suggestion to him?

a. you should eat more.

b. you should study harder

c. I think you ought to get some rest

d. what if you go for a walk?

jawaban: c

2. Linda is working on her homework, but she finds some difficult questions. What will you suggest her?

a. I think you should stop studying.

b. maybe you can sleep now.

c. What if you drink to refresh yourself?

d. what about going to your friend's house to study together?

jawaban: d

3. A: .... go to the cinema and watch a good film?

B: That's a good idea

a. you must

b. you have

c. I think you should

d. what if we

jawaban: d

4. A: I feel drowsy.

B: ....

a. you should take a rest.

b. what if you study harder?

c. what about calling your friend for help?

d. you had better call the police.

jawaban: a

5. This is Saturday night. What will you suggest your friend?

a. I think we should go to the town square for sight seeing.

b. what about going to the doctor?

c. you should speak to your teacher.

d. what if we take this to be repaired?

jawaban: a

6. Doctor: .... smoking if you don't want to get sick.

Patient: Yes sir, I will try.

a. what if you stop

b. you should stop

c. how about

d. maybe you can stop

jawaban: b

7. A: I am so hungry.

B: ....

a. I think you should take a rest.

b. You ought to read it more carefully.

c. Calm down! You should be more relaxed.

d. What about going to a restaurant?

jawaban: d

8. Mother: I want to cook an omelette.

You: ....

a. Let's buy some eggs.

b. Let's buy some syrup.

c. I think you should buy a ticket first.

d. I think you should go to a barbershop.

jawaban: a

9. Your friend needs a book for his homework. But he doesn't have enough money to buy it. What will you suggest him?

a. Maybe you can borrow from our seniors?

b. I think you shouldn't do the homework.

c. What if we go to the zoo?

d. You ought to get some rest.

jawaban: a

10. You and your friends are on a trip to a beach in Banyuwangi. However, none of you knows about the route the that beach. What will be your suggestion?

a. I think we can go home now.

b. What about going to a restaurant?

c. Maybe you can open Google Maps application on your phone.

d. You should read the instruction more carefully.

jawaban: c
