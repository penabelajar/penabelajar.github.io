Jawaban:

(–) A dog isn't an animal.

(?) Is a dog an animal?

(–) A potato isn't a vegetable.

(?) Is A potato a vegetable?

(–) I'm not always good.

(?) Am I always good?

(–) A chair isn't made of wood.

(?) Is a chair made of wood?

(–) The aren't hungry.

(?) Are they hungry?

(–) The cows aren't tame animals.

(?) Are the cows tame animals?

7. (+) He is my grandfather.

(–) He isn't my grandfather

(?) Is he my grandfather?

8. (+) We are family.

(–) We aren't family.

(?) Are we family?

(–) A novel isn't a book.

(?) Is a novel a book?
