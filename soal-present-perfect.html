15. present perfect dan present perfect continuos​


Jawaban:

-Present Perfect (I have read) menyatakan pemikiran tentang sesuatu yang telah selesai/sudah selesai dilakukan. Present Perfect Continuous menyatakan sesuatu belum selesai dilakukan.

-Present Perfect Continuous (has been writing) menyatakan seberapa lama sesuatu terjadi.

Contoh:

Present Perfect Continoues:

-I just follow the cooking guide and I have baking the cake for 30 minutes

Present Perfect:

-I have bought a new car.
