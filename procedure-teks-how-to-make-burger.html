How to Make a Cup of Black Coffee.

Ingredients:

1. a tablespoon of black coffee powder.
2. sugar.
3. a half teaspoon of chocolate powder.

Steps:

1. Heat water for a medium cup.
2. If the water is already boiled, add the coffee powder and stir it. After that boil it again and add chocolate powder.
3. Prepare the coffee filter (a soft cloth) and two medium mugs.
4. Pour the hot black coffee and filter it.
5. Prepare a cup which is already given sugar and pour the black coffee by using the filter again.
6. Finally, the black coffee is ready to serve. It is the best to enjoy with churros, macaroon, or the other snacks.how to make coffee

How to Make Bakwan

How to Make

materials used for How to Make Bakwan:

- 200 grams of sweet corn

- 4 eggs

- 3 tablespoons flour

- 1 stalk spring onion, sliced

14. procedure text how to make puding


How to Make Chocolate pudding
Material :
- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack
- Milk 400 Ml Liquid
- 50 gr Dark Cooking Chocolate, Chopped
- 2 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 

Tools:Pan, Bowl, Stove, Spoon, Mold, Refrigerator, Water
Steps:

First, Agar-agar cook with milk, chocolate and sugar over medium heat until hot.
Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.
Next, Beaten egg whites until fluffy and up. Then pour the hot gelatin mixture gradually while stirring constantly until well blended. 
Then, Save or pour into a mold. Allow it to freeze, so quickly refrigerate until cold. 
Finally, prepared pudding served with a complement.
