1. How to make coffee milk - Procedure Text


How to make coffee milk

Ingredients

One tablespoon of ground coffeeOne glass of hot waterMilk

Steps:

Prepare a 250 ml glass of water to a boil. Then add the coffee grounds to the water. Wait until it boils while stirring using a spoon. When the water containing the coffee grounds has boiled then pour it into the glass that has been provided and stir slowly and add milk. Milk Coffee is ready to be served. If necessary, add cream so that the bitter taste of the coffee is not too strong.

Serving suggestion: drink the brewed coffee milk without sugar so that the coffee becomes healthy and the coffee aroma is stronger.

6. procedure text how to make puding


How to Make Chocolate pudding
Material :
- Agar-Agar White 1 Pack
- Milk 400 Ml Liquid
- 50 gr Dark Cooking Chocolate, Chopped
- 2 Tablespoons Granulated Sugar
- 1 Egg Yolk, Beaten
- Egg Whites 3 Eggs 

Tools:Pan, Bowl, Stove, Spoon, Mold, Refrigerator, Water
Steps:

First, Agar-agar cook with milk, chocolate and sugar over medium heat until hot.
Second, take a little dough, then mix with the egg yolks, pour back into the hot mixture. Cook until boiling. Lift.
Next, Beaten egg whites until fluffy and up. Then pour the hot gelatin mixture gradually while stirring constantly until well blended. 
Then, Save or pour into a mold. Allow it to freeze, so quickly refrigerate until cold. 
Finally, prepared pudding served with a complement.

8. contoh procedure text how to make a glass of coffee


Contoh procedure text how to make a glass (cup) of coffee:

How to make a glass of coffee:

Ingredients

1 teaspoon of coffee 1½ spoons of cream½ teaspoon of sugar A cup of water (300 ml)

Steps:

First, heat 300 ml of water until it boils. Mix the coffee grounds and cream into the cup. After the water boils, pour it into the cup containing coffee and cream. Stir gently until the coffee dissolves in the water and the cream mixes with the coffee. The color of the coffee will be brown. It is recommended not to add sugar, but if you want to, add half a teaspoon of white sugar. The coffee is ready to be served.

Pembahasan

Teks tersebut terdiri dari dua bagian, yaitu bagian ingredients (bahan-bahan) dan bagian steps (langkah-langkah pembuatan). Kamu bisa menambahkan bahan-bahan lain ke dalam teks di atas, atau menambah langkah-langkah lainnya.

3. Prepare the coffee filter (a soft cloth) and two medium mugs.
4. Pour the hot black coffee and filter it.

10. text procedure dari how to make soup


How To Make Chicken Soup
Ingredients :

1 (3 pound) whole chicken     
 4 carrots, halved
4 stalks celery, halved
1 large onion, halved
 water to cover salt and pepper to taste
1 teaspoon chicken bouillon granules



1.Directions Put the chicken, carrots, celery and onion in a large soup pot and cover with cold water. Heat and simmer, uncovered, until the chicken meat falls off of the bones (skim off foam every so often).
2.Take everything out of the pot. Strain the broth. Pick the meat off of the bones and chop the carrots, celery and onion. Season the broth with salt, pepper and chicken bouillon to taste, if desired. Return the chicken, carrots, celery and onion to the pot, stir together, and serve.

How to make a cup of coffee

Aim : To know how to make a cup of coffe

Materials : – a spoon of coffee

– some sugar

– a glass of water

– some chocolate (optional, may use or not)

Steps :

1. Boil a glass of water in the medium heat, until the water becomes hot enough.

2. Put a spoon of coffe in a cup.

3. Add some sugar to the cup, based on your necesserity.

4. Add some chocolate to the cup. (optional, may add or not)

5. After the water had already boiled, pour the water and the coffe in the cup.



6. Mix it together until all of the materials are poured.

7. A cup of hot delicious coffee is ready to serve.

How to Make Bakwan

How to Make

materials used for How to Make Bakwan:

- 200 grams of sweet corn

- 4 eggs

- 3 tablespoons flour

- 1 stalk spring onion, sliced

- 100 grams of carrots, cut into matchsticks salt and pepper to taste

- 100 grams of shrimp (can be cut or placed on bakwan)

- 500 ml cooking oil

How to Make Bakwan :

- Combine corn, shrimp, egg, flour, scallions, bean sprouts, carrots, salt, and pepper.

- Heat the oil. Add 1 ladle into it, after the heat, pour batter into a ladle bakwan, place the shrimp on it.

- Fry until golden brown bakwan.
