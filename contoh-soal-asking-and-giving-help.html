6. contoh dialog asking giving and refusing help


Asking
Lala : Hi, Laila. Can you help me?
Laila : Sure. What can I do for you?
Lala : Would you get my English book in my classroom for me, please?
Laila : Okay.

Giving
Dhika : Hello, Putri. May I help you?
Putri : Sure. Would you lend your money for me, please?
Dhika : How much?
Putri : Five dollars.
Dhika : Sure. Here it is.
Putri : Thank you.
Dhika : No problem.

Refusing
Fany : Hello, Mike.
Mike : Hello, Fany.
Fany : Mike, I want to invite you to go to picnic this weekend, can you join?
Mike : Sounds great. Actually I want to go. But, sorry Mike, I have promised my friend to go to the mall.
Fany : Oh, that’s okay, Fany.
Mike : Have a nice picnic!
Fany : Thanks Mike!
Mike : Anytime!
