Rujak (Indonesian spelling) or Rojak (Malay spelling) is a salad dish of Javanese origin, commonly found in Indonesia, Malaysia and Singapore.Rujak is a salad composed of a mixture of sliced fruit and vegetables served with a spicy palm sugar dressing.In Indonesia there is also Rujak Cingur.Rujak Cingur ia vegetables or fruit salad with slices of beef snout.Rujak is often described as tangy and spicy fruit salad due to its sweet, hot and spicy dressing made from ground chilli, palm sugar and peanuts.

Translate ke Bahasa Indonesia : Rujak (dalam ejaan Bahasa Indonesia) atau Rojak (dalam ejaan Bahasa Melayu) adalah hidangan salad asal Jawa, yang biasa ditemukan di Indonesia, Malaysia, dan Singapura. Rujak adalah salad yang terdiri dari campuran irisan buah dan sayuran yang disajikan dengan saus gula aren pedas. Di Indonesia juga ada Rujak Cingur.Rujak Cingur berupa sayuran atau salad buah dengan irisan moncong daging sapi.Rujak sering digambarkan sebagai salad buah yang tajam dan pedas karena sausnya yang manis, panas dan pedas yang terbuat dari cabai giling, gula aren dan kacang tanah.

6. cara bikin rujak pake bahasa inggris gmana?


Rujak Buah - Fruit with Spicy Palm Sugar Sauce


Ingredients

Rujak Sauce

200 gram palm sugar (Indonesian: gula Jawa), shaved

15 gram tamarind flesh + 5 tablespoon drinking water, strained

6 (or more) Thai chilies, seeded

1/4 teaspoon toasted shrimp paste (Indonesian: terasi)

1/4 teaspoon salt

Typical Fruits for Rujak

jicama (Indonesian: bengkoang)

starfruit (Indonesian: belimbing)

cucumber (Indonesian: timun)

water apple (Indonesian: jambu air)

pineapple (Indonesian: nenas)

ambarella (Indonesian: kedondong)

mango (Indonesian: mangga)

unripe papaya (Indonesian: pepaya yang belum matang)

Instructions

In a food processor/blender, process all rujak sauce ingredients into a thick gooey liquid. Transfer to a dipping bowl.

Wash and cut some of the suggested typical fruits and arrange in a serving plate.

Serve both the cut fruits and rujak sauce together. Dip the cut fruit into the sauce prior to eating.


7. bagaimana cara Membuat Rujak Serut Dalam Bahasa Inggris Dan terjemahannya​


Jawaban:

Bahasa Indonesia :

Bahan :

200 g bengkuang

250g mangga muda

150 g pepaya mengkal

150 g kedondong

250 g nanas

400 g gula merah

250 ml air, untuk merebus gula

1 buah pisang batu muda, iris tipis

2 sdm kacang tanah goreng

2 sdm air asam jawa

100 ml air matang

es batu (bila suka)

Bumbu

3 buah cabai rawit

1 sdt terasi matang

2 sdt garam

Cara membuat:

1. Kupas bengkuang, mangga, pepaya, dan kedondong, cuci bersih. Serut kasar masing-masing buah, sisihkan. Kupas nanas, lalu potong-potong kecil, sisihkan.

2. Iris tipis/sisir gula merah, lalu taruh dalam panci. Tambahkan air, jerang di atas api, rebus hingga gula larut, angkat, biarkan dingin. Pindahkan larutan gula ke dalam wadah/mangkuk.

3. Gerus bahan bumbu menggunakan cobek hingga halus. Tambahkan pisang batu dan kacang tanah, gerus kasar-kasar. Masukkan air asam jawa dan air matang, lalu campur hingga rata. Masukkan campuran bumbu ke dalam air gula, aduk hingga tercampur rata.

4. Tambahkan buah serut ke dalam campuran gula dan bumbu, aduk. Simpan dalam lemari es menunggu waktu akan dihidangkan agar bumbu meresap.

5. Pindahkan rujak ke dalam mangkuk saji, hidangkan dingin. Bila suka tambahkan es batu.

Bahasa Inggris :

material :

200 g yam

250g young mangoes

150 g papal mengkal

150 g of kedondong

250 g of pineapple

400 g brown sugar

250 ml of water, to boil sugar

1 young stone banana, thinly sliced

2 tablespoons fried peanuts

2 tbsp tamarind water

100 ml of boiled water

ice cube (if you like)

Seasoning :

3 cayenne peppers

1 tsp cooked shrimp paste

2 teaspoons of salt

How to make:

1. Peel the yam, mango, papaya, and kedondong, wash it clean. Coarsely shaved each fruit, set aside. Peel a pineapple, then cut into small pieces, set aside.

2. Thinly slice / comb the brown sugar, then put it in a pan. Add water, steam on the fire, boil until the sugar has dissolved, remove from heat, let it cool. Transfer the sugar solution into a container / bowl.

3. Grind the ingredients using a mortar until smooth. Add rock bananas and peanuts, grind roughly. Add tamarind water and boiled water, then mix until blended. Enter the seasoning mixture into sugar water, stir until evenly mixed.

4. Add shaved fruit to the sugar and seasoning mixture, stirring. Store in the refrigerator waiting for the time to be served so that the spices seep.

5. Transfer the salad to a serving bowl, serve cold. If you like add ice cubes.
