Word Order Subject + Verb + Object :

• Linda ate 2 cookies last night.
• I hate Eddy.
• Kiki ia playing football
• She bought a new car
• Siska plays piano
• Our headmaster calls you.
• Ani buys Shinchan comic books
• We will reveal the truth.
• We will watch that movie
• That boy throw his bottle.
Berikut contoh-contoh kalimat dengan word order Subject + Verb + Object:

1. Linda ate 2 cookies last night.
(Linda makan 2 biskuit semalam.)

Subject: Linda
Verb: eats
Object : 2 cookies
Adverb of Time: last night

2.  I hate Eddy.
(Saya membenci Eddy.)

Subject: I
Verb: hate
Object: Eddy

3. Kiki is playing football.
(Kiki sedang bermain sepakbola)

Subject: Kiki
Verb: is playing
Object: football

4. She bought a new car
(Ia membeli sebuah mobil baru.)

Subject: She
Verb: bought
Object: a new car

5. Siska plays piano
(Siska bermain piano.)

Subject: Siska
Verb: plays
Object: piano

6. Our headmaster calls you.
(Kepala sekolah kita memanggil kamu.)

Subject: Our headmaster
Verb: calls
Object: you

7. Ani buys Shinchan comic books
(Ani membeli buku komik Shinchan.)

Subject: Ani
Verb: buys
Object: Shinchan comic books.

8. We will reveal the truth.
(Kami akan mengungkapkan kebenarannya.)

Subject: We
Verb: will reveal
Object: the truth

9. We will watch that movie.
(Kami akan menonton film itu.)

Subject: We
Verb: will watch
Object: that movie

10. That boy throw his bottle.
(Anak laki-laki itu melempar botolnya.)

Subject: That boy
Verb: throw
Object: his bottle

11. Selina loves Kevin
(Selina mencintai Kevin.)

Subject: Selina
Verb: loves
Object: Kevin

12. Lucy betrays me.
(Lucy mengkhianati saya)

Subject: Lucy
Verb: betrays
Object: me

13. My father fetches me.
(Ayah saya menjemput saya.)

Subject: My father
Verb: fetches
Object: me

14. Little Jack drops that cake.
(Jack kecil menjatuhkan kue itu.)

Subject: Little Jack
Verb: drops
Object: that cake

15. My brother buys roses.
