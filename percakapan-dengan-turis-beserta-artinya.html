A : How long have you been here? (Sudah berapa lama anda disini?)

B : i have been in jakarta for a week (saya sudah di jakarta sejak seminggu yang lalu)

A : Cool, enjoy jakarta, thank you for visiting indonesia (Keren, selamat menikmati jakarta, terimakasih sudah berkunjung ke indonesia)
