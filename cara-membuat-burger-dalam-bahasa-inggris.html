5. buatkan resep membuat roti burger dalam bahasa inggris


. Beef Mushroom Burger

Materials:

1.    2 tbsp. of butter
2.    60 grams onion, chopped
3.    150 grams mushrooms, cut into small
4.    300 grams of minced beef
5.    2 tbsp. of wheat flour
6.    2 eggs, take the yolk
7.    1½ tsp. of pepper powder
8.    ½ tsp. of nutmeg
9.    1 tsp. of salt

Supplementary Material Burger:

1.    Barbeque sauce
2.    Vegetable salad
3.    French fries

How to make Mushroom Beef burger:

1.    Sauté onion and mushrooms, stir until wilted and browned. Then Remove and chill.
2.    Combine all ingredients into a small bowl and stir until the dough is smooth.
3.    Mix the dough with the mushrooms that have been sautéed until blended.
4.    Shape the dough into a flat circle.
5.    Heat a frying pan, melt the butter and cook burgers until cooked on both sides. Then remove and set aside.
6.    Cook the minced beef until cooked and then shape into a flat form.
7.    Put the burger between two buns and serve with its complementary ingredients.

10. Jelaskan asal usul makanan burger dalam bahasa Inggris


Many people are mistaken and think that the name Hamburger comes from the word "Ham", but actually the name comes from the city of Hamburg in Germany, where this food originated.

From the second largest city in Germany, many residents emigrated to America and spread burger making there. It's just a coincidence that the word "ham" which in English means smoked meat has a sound that is almost similar to Hamburger, in fact the hamburger does not contain Ham (although there are also restaurants that add Ham slices to their burgers to taste).

So it can be concluded literally that the meaning of the word Hamburger means "food originating from Hamburg" and does not mean "food containing Ham". But in practice burger or hamburger is more often interpreted as a sandwich or other type of round sandwich. In society the word burger is more inherent as a type of food than the origin and creator of the burger.

12. resep burger dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


Procedure Text

JAWABAN:

HOW TO MAKE BURGER

INGREDIENTS

Burger

Around 500 g of ground beef 6 bun burgers 1 egg yolk

Seasoning

½ onion Tomato sauce 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire sauce 1 tablespoon of mustard 1 tablespoon of white pepper 1 clove of garlic A little fresh spice leaves, sliced ​​roughly Pepper and salt

Topping

2 fresh tomatoes, thinly sliced 6 pieces of cheese Lettuce Tomato sauce Mayonnaise

STEPS

Part 1

How to Make Burger Patti

Choose the right meat. Ask the butcher to grind beef containing 15% fat. If you use more fat, this fat will only drip from meat and cause the fire to become more intense so the burger will dry. If possible, buy meat on the day you cook it.  Ask the butcher to grind the meat of your choice twice. Once with a coarse grind and then with a finer grind. Place ground beef in a bowl. You will add other ingredients to it when it's ready. Roughly chop onions and garlic. Enter into a bowl and mix until smooth. Add other ingredients that you want to add to the burger. Including Worcestershire sauce, tomato sauce, mustard, or seasoned leaf slices. These ingredients can be added according to your taste, but these ingredients can certainly add flavor to your homemade burger. Add egg yolk. Season one egg yolk with pepper and salt, mix until blended. Mix with a spoon, then continue using your clean hands until all the ingredients are thoroughly mixed. Make a burger. Hold the meat as little as possible, so that not much fat comes out. Shape the burger into 6 equal sized meat balls using your hands. Press the meat ball down to make it evenly thick (approximately 1.27cm). Make a small basin in the middle of the burger with your thumb. This will make the middle of the burger bulge, so the burger is not evenly cooked.

Part 2

How to Cook Burgers

Place the burger on a plate. Cover with plastic or  proof paper. Chill for 30 minutes to several hours to make it stiffer so it's easier to cook. Burger meat is best cooked when it's cold.Preheat the grill (the top) to medium heat. Cover the dish used for baking with foil, so that it is easier to clean up after finishing. Place the burger in the dish. Bake for 6-7 minutes on each side until cooked evenly. Place the burger in the barbecue grill. Cook as you normally cook hamburgers with barbecues.Roast in the oven: Place the burger in the oven at 350ºF / 180ºC for 15 to 30 minutes, depending on the thickness. Turn your burger over after cooking half of it, and check its maturity for some time. While waiting for your burger to cook, prepare the topping. You can choose whatever toppings you like, and here is a selection of traditional burger toppings that you can consider:  Wash lettuce and tomatoes and thinly sliced ​​tomatoes.   Prepare tomato sauce and mayonnaise on the dining table according to taste. Serve. After the burger is cooked, serve the burger. Place the topping in the burger bun and serve it on the dining table. Alternatively, place the burger on a plate with other foods such as rice, potato chips, mashed mussels, or salad.
