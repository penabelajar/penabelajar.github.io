13. greetings and Introduction​


Pengertian Greeting :

Greeting adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyapa seseorang. Situasi dalam greeting terdiri dari dua bagian yaitu formal dan informal.

1. Contoh Greeting Formal :

Hello! (Halo!)Good morning. (Selamat pagi)Good afternoon. (Selamat siang)Good evening. (Selamat sore)Good night. (Selamat malam)Hello, how are you? (Halo apa kabarmu?)How are you doing? (Apa kabarmu?)How do you do? (Kalimat sapaan jika kita belum pernah bertemu dengan orang itu sebelumnya)Nice to meet you. (Senang bertemu denganmu)

2. Contoh Greeting informal :

Hi! (Hai!)Hey? (Hei) What’s up? (Apa kabar?) What’s news? (Apa yang baru?)How’s everything? (Bagaimana semuanya?)How’s it going? (Bagaimana sekarang?)How’s business? (Bagaimana bisnis?)Good to see you. (Senang bertemu kamu)

Pengertian Introduction :

Adalah ungkapan/ekspresi yang digunakan untuk perkenalan atau memperkenalkan diri dalam bahasa Inggris. Dalam bahasa Inggris, perkenalan diri disebut juga dengan introduction. hal-hal yang umum disampaikan ketika mengenalkan diri di antaranya :

Nama/Name

Asal/Country

Usia/Age

Keluarga/Family

Ulang tahun/Birthday

Pekerjaan/Jobs

Hobi/Hobbies

Contoh Introduction (Formal) :

Good morning, everyone. i would like to introduce about myself.

My name is Caitlyn Hudson and I am 13 years old. I was born on third December 2007.

I'm a transfer student from North Carolina, United States.

I am currently live in Jakarta with my parent and my sister.

My hobbies are skateboarding and dancing.

Nice to meet you!

Contoh Introduction (informal) :

Hey, I'm William Parker, you can call me Will for short.

I'm from North Carolina, but I'm currently stay in

Jakarta for the student exchange at this school.

i am 13 years old for now.

i enjoy dancing and skateboarding.

thanks, good to see you.

15. What do dito and andre say to respond to andi and lusi's introduction? ​


Jawaban:

Kelas : X

Pelajaran : bahasa Inggris

Kategori : complimenting and congratulating

Kata kunci : Congratulations

Pembahasan:

Pada buku kelas X, nama dialog dengan nama yang tertulis di pertanyaan antara Cita dan Dita. Saya samakan dengan nama di pertanyaan, yaitu Dita.

1. What good news about Dita does Ditto know? 

Ditto knows good news about Dita has won the first winner of the story telling competition in her school.

2. What does Ditto say to Dita related to the news? 

Ditto says, "Congratulations for being the first winner of the school story telling competition! Excellent. You really did it well."

3. What do the expressions mean? 

The expressions mean Ditto is happy with Dita's achievement.

4. What is Ditto's purpose of saying that to Dita? 

Ditto's purpose of saying that to Dita is to express his happiness about Dita's achievement as the first winner of the school story telling competition.

5. How does Dita respond to what Ditto says? 

Dita responds to what Ditto says gratefully and happily.

6. When do you think you will say 'congratulations' to other people?

I think I will say 'congratulations' to other  people when we hear good news about someone's achievement, promotion, graduation, etc.
