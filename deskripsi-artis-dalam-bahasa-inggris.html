Menggunakan simple presentMenggunakan action verb, yaitu kata kerja yang menunjukkan suatu kegiatan yang bisa dilihat seperti walk, sleep, read, write dan lain sebagainya.Verb yang digunakan adalah attribute verb seperti be (am, is, dan are)

Leonardo DiCaprio

Do you know the movie called Titanic? If the answer is yes, then you surely know who's the actor who plays jack in the film is! Yes, he is one of the greatest actor in his generation, Leonardo DiCaprio.

Leonardo Wilhelm DiCaprio known as Leonardo DiCaprio was born on November 11, 1974 in Los Angeles, California, United States of America. His height is 183 cm. He has blue eyes and blonde hair. He is the only child of his parents, Imerlin and George DiCaprio. When he was a year old, his parents divorced and he lived with his mother in poor neighborhood. However, Leonardo still met his father. His father was an underground writer, he liked to read comic books and went to the museum with his father. Ever since he was a kid, his parents had seen his acting talent in him. So, his parents sent him to talent agent to improve his ability in acting.

In 1991 after he graduated from John Marshall High School, he started his debut in a horror film "Critters 3". Not long after that, in 1992 he managed to get the main role in the film "This Boy's Life". In 1993 he won an Oscar and a Golden Globe Nomination for Best Supporting Actor for his role as Johnny Depp's brother in "What's Eating Gilbert Grape". In span of 2 years, 1995-1997, Leonardo managed to get roles in several famous films, such as "The Quick and The Dead", "The Basketball Diaries", "Romeo and Juliet" and "Titanic" which made his name as an actor skyrocketed.
