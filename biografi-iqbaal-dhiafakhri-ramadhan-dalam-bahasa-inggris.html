5. Buatlah biografi tentang Iqbaal CJR


Biografi

Iqbaal Dhiafakhri Ramadhan atau dikenal dengan Iqbal Cowboy Junior lahir di Surabaya, 28 Desember 1999 adalah aktor dan penyanyi berkebangsaan Indonesia anggota boy band, Coboy Junior. Karier Iqbaal di dunia hiburan diawali saat berperan sebagai Trapani dalam, Musikal Laskar Pelangi (2010-2011). Tahun 2011 Iqbaal mulai bermain di Layar Lebar dalam film berjudul “5 Elang” sebagai Rusdi Badrudin.

Tahun 2013, Iqbaal bersama anggota Coboy Junior berperan di film Coboy Junior The Movie. Film itu terbilang sukses  dan berhasil menembus angka 500 ribu penonton. Film itu juga dibintangi oleh  Nirina Zubir, Dewi Sandra, dan Meisya Siregar.

Awal tahun 2014, Iqbaal bersama Cowboy Junior kembali berperan di film aksi komedu Comic 8. Meski Kiky, Aldi, Bastiandan Iqbal hanya menjadi cameo di menit-menit awal film mereka mengaku cukup puas.

Tahun 2014 ada kabar yang menyebut bahwa Iqbal dan Kiki hengkang dari Coboy Junior. Kabar itu ditampik santai oleh boy band berpersonil cilik ini.Menurut mereka, hingga saat ini Iqbal dan Kiki masih tetap bergabung, Coboy Junior justru makin solid.

iqbaale cjr lahir pada tanggal 28 desember 1999, dia merupakan murid dari GIS ( Global Islamic School), ia merupakan personel CJR yang memiliki bakat menyanyi yang baik, ia juga selalu memiliki nilai yang bagus.

Ahmad: Amin. Karena Ramadhan penuh dengan godaan. Kita harus lebih sabar.

Ahmad: Aku akan mengajak kamu berbuka puasa dan makan malam bersama keluargaku di hari pertama Ramadhan. Apakah kamu mau datang?

Ali: Insya Allah. Ramadan pertama akan diadakan pada hari Rabu, 16 Mei 2018. Apakah saya benar?

Ahmad: Yes, you are. Will you help your mother to make Lebaran cakes?

Ahmad: Ya, kamu benar. Maukah kamu membantu ibumu untuk membuat kue Lebaran?

Ali: Nope. My sister will do that. That’s a women business. I will clean the house as the symbol that I clean my self to be a better person.

Ali: Tidak. Adikku akan melakukan itu. Itu urusan wanita. Aku akan membersihkan rumah sebagai simbol bahwa aku membersihkan diriku untuk menjadi orang yang lebih baik.

Ahmad: Wow! That’s great.

Ahmad: Wow! Itu hebat.

Ali: BTW. It’s almost Dzuhur. Let’s prepare for Mosque.

Ali: Ngomong-ngomong. Sudah hampir Dzuhur. Mari bersiap ke Masjid.

Ahmad: Let’s go.

13. tulisakan kisah biografi iqbaal diafakhri ramadan dg bahasa inggris


Kisah biografi Iqbaal Ramadhan dalam bahasa Inggris adalah sebagai berikut.


Pembahasan

Iqbaal Ramadhan is a singer and actor who was born in Surabaya on December 29th 1999. He is originally from Bandung but now lives in East Jakarta. Iqbal is a son from his father Herry Hernawan and mother Rike Dhamayanti who gave him full name Iqbaal Dhiafakhri Ramadhan. However he’s well known by Iqbaal Ramadhan or only Iqbaal.


Iqbaal went to Kindergarten of TKIT Al Izzah in Sorong, Papua (2005-2006), and later he continued his school to Elementary school SDIT Al Izzah in Sorong, Papua. On 2007 he moved to SDIT Al Muhajirin Pondok Kopi until 2012. Iqbaal enter the Middle school in SMP Global Islamic School Condet (2012-2015), High school SMA Global Islamic School Condet (2015-2016), and now he’s studying in Armand Hammer United World College of the American West, Montezuma, New Mexico, USA (2016-2018).


He started his career and join in musical drama Laskar Pelangi on 2010-2011, he played as Trapani. After that, Iqbaal joined with the kids boybad Coboy Junior with his fellows, Bastian, Kiki and Aldi. He became famous since then.


Other than performing with his boyband, Iqbaal personally also launched two mini albums which is ‘song for SoniQ 1’ at the year 2013 and ‘song for SoniQ II’ at the year 2014, and his single “Untitled Song” released on 2013. Iqbaal also wrote a song under the title “Semesta” which released as a part of the mini album “Song for SoniQ III” 2016 last year.


Other than singing, Iqbaal also known as an actor. His first debut was 5 Elang on 2011, he played as Rusdi Badruddin. After that, Iqbaal’s career is improving, he also played in Coboy Junor The Movie, Ada Cinta di SMA and the latest Dilan 1990 as Dilan. Iqbaal also played in Indonesian’s soap opera (sinetron) called Hanya Kamu.


Iqbaal has many fans, majority of them are girls. They call themselves as SoniQ; the abbreviation of “Sobatnya Iqbaal”, literally translates to Iqbaal’s friends.
