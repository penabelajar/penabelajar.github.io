Jawaban: The problem underlying this research is the low ability of students to write fable texts. This is caused by the lack of student interest in participating in learning and the lack of creativity of teachers when using instructional media. This study aims to determine the improvement of the process and results of writing skills of fable text using the image media of Grade VII students of SMP Negeri 1 Bandar Lampung in the 2019/2020 Academic Year. This Classroom Action Research consists of two cycles, namely cycle I and II. Data collection methods used are observation methods, test methods (in the form of assignments to write fables), field notes methods, and documentation methods. Based on the results of the learning process obtained the following results. The results of the increase in teacher activity in the first cycle reached 77%, increasing to 86% in the second cycle. In the first cycle of student learning activities namely 51% increased to 69% in the second cycle. Student grades have also increased. The average student learning outcomes in the first cycle reached 67%, increasing to 78% in the second cycle. Thus, it can be concluded that the use of image media can improve the quality of the process and learning outcomes of writing fable text for seventh grade students of SMP Negeri 1 Bandar Lampung, 2019/2020 Academic Year
