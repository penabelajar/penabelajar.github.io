2. pidato corona anak sd​


Jawaban:

To all my friends, good day! We all realize that the coronavirus is a serious global pandemic. The size of the virus is extremely small, that it is not visible to the human eyes, and can enter our body and destroy our immune system, and will get us infected anytime and anywhere as a result. The coronavirus was first brought from Wuhan, China and now it's an ongoing global pandemic. Up until today there are still many people who still ignore the safety protocols, claiming that they feel healthy and vigorous, unaware of the small virus which could attack their bodies.

As future generations, as students, we can't be as neglectful as those people! The vaccination may be still in progress and has made the number of new cases go constant without any steep increment or decrease. However the number of new daily cases is still above the '1000 cases' threshold, and it makes others who obey the safety protocols, sacrifice their health and postpone any activity because of being accidentally infected.

Always remember to use your mask every time you go out, tightly press the part on your nose and ensure your whole cheek and mouth area is covered so that the spread of the virus can be lessen. Wash your hands with soap and water as frequent as possible, and also keep your distance from people around you so that the saliva droplet does not reach your nose and could cause Covid-19 infection.

Coronavirus, tiny, but it's just everywhere. Silently attacking. Don't lose against the 'virus monarchy', as the people who didn't obey the protocols eventually got infected. Be considerate. Start living healthy from now on!

Thank you for your attention, good day.

Penjelasan:

Teks pidato diawali dengan

pembukaan, topik, breakdown topik serta ajakan persuasif dan jelas.

diakhiri dengan

conclusion/kesimpulan dan ajakan untuk melakukan sesuatu yg berhubungan dengan topik.

Artinya:

Salam sejahtera untuk kita semua. Teman-temanku semua, kita semua tahu bahwa virus Corona itu adalah pandemi dunia yang menggawatkan. Ukuran virus Corona sangat kecil, tidak terlihat oleh mata, dan bisa masuk kedalam tubuh kita dan berpengaruh terhadap imunitas tubuh sehingga kita bisa tertular kapan saja dan dimana saja. Virus Corona pertama dibawa dari Wuhan dan sekarang menjadi pandemi global. Sampai kini banyak warga yang abai dengan protokol kesehatan karena merasa mereka sehat bugar, namun tidak mengetahui virus itu diam-diam menggerogoti tubuh mereka.

Sebagai generasi masa depan bangsa, kita sebagai siswa jangan mau ikut teledor seperti warga-warga yang lain. Meskipun vaksinasi sudah gencar dilakukan dan membuat jumlah kasus baru konstan, namun jumlah kasus harian masihlah diatas ribuan dan membuat orang-orang lain yang patuh, mengorbankan kesehatan dan menunda aktivitas karena tertular tanpa sengaja.

Jangan lupa, selalu gunakan masker setiap keluar rumah, ketatkan bagian batang hidung dan tutupilah mulut agar penularan tidak terjadi. Cucilah tangan dengan sabun dan air mengalir sesering mungkin. Serta menjaga jarak dari orang-orang disekitar agar droplet ludah tidak menyebar dan menyebabkan penularan virus Corona.

Virus Corona, kecil, tapi dimana-mana, dan diam-diam menggerogoti. Jangan kalah seperti orang-orang yang tidak mau patuh dengan protokol kesehatan. Jangan lengah. Mulailah hidup sehat dari sekarang! Terima kasih.

Dear My Teachers and My friends,

I want to tell you everything I know about corona virus or covid19. It is a dangerous virus. We have to be safe from it. We have to stop covid19 from spreading. How do we stop covid19?

We have follow our health protocol. We have to wear a mask whenever we go outside. We have to wash our hands with soap as often as possible. We have to stay at home and support the distance learning programs from our school. We have to eat nutritious food every day. We must bask in the morning sunlight to help improve the immune system in our body.

If we do all that, covid19 will stop spreading and everyone will be a hero. Thank you.

Kita harus mengikuti protokol kesehatan. Kita harus memakai masker setiap kali kita pergi keluar. Kita harus mencuci tangan dengan sabun sesering mungkin. Kita harus tinggal di rumah dan mendukung program pembelajaran jarak jauh dari sekolah kita. Kita harus makan makanan bergizi setiap hari. Kita harus berjemur di bawah sinar matahari pagi untuk membantu meningkatkan sistem kekebalan dalam tubuh kita.
