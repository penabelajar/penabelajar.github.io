11. soal pilihan ganda langsung kunci jawabannya tentang introduction self


SELF INTRODUCTION

Self introduction adalah suatu aktivitas memperkenalkan diri kita sendiri kepada orang lain. Biasanya dilakukan ketika kita bertemu pertama kali dengan seseorang.

Tergantung kepada situasi, kadang-kadang dalam memperkenalkan diri biasanya kita hanya menyebutkan nama, namun pada situasi tertentu kita memperkenalkan diri lebih dari sekedar nama, terutama dalam situasi formal.

Beberapa informasi yang biasanya diberikan dalam self introduction :

name/nick namewhere do you come fromaddress/where do you liveyour hobbynumber of people in the familyyour favorite food, drink, games, etcyour ageyour occupationPembahasan

1.These situation below need a self introduction, except:

  a. When we meet some new people

  b. In an interview

  c. When meeting your best friend

  d. The beginning of school year

  a. Favorite color, pet's name, how many people in the family

  b. Name, nick name, address, where I live

  c. Age, Favorite food and drink, hobbies

  d. All above

--Question about Introducing Self--
1) Hello, guys! Let me .... myself. My name is Hanna. I come from Bekasi. Nice to meet you.
The correct word to fill the blanks is ...
==> introduce

2) The correct expression to introducing self is ...
==> Let me introduce myself

3) Hello, please meet my cousin. ... name is Ghani.
The correct pronoun is ...
==> His

4) Chaca-cousin-is-she-named-my
The correct arrangement is ...
==> She is my cousin named Chaca

5) Things that usually used in a introducing text are ..
==> name, birth date, age, class, address, hobby, etc

6) The expression that usually uses in the end of the introucing text is ...
==> Nice to meet you / Let's be friends // etc

7) The purpose of introducing ourselves is ...
==> to let people knows about us

8) ... My name is Zahra.
The correct greetings is ...
==> Hello / Hi / Good morning

9) My name is Alfi. I just ... here two days ago. Let's be friends
The correct verb is ...
==> moved

10) Through introducing self, we can ... friends and ... more people
The correct words is
==> make, know
