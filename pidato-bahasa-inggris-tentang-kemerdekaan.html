2. Teks pidato bahasa inggris tentang kemerdekaan


Assalamualaikum Wr. Wb. , Good morning and best wishes for all of us

Ladies and gentlemen, whom I respect and my colleagues who I am proud and honored.

First of all, let us offer puja and thank God the Almighty. I thank you very much for giving me the opportunity to say a few words with the theme "Republic of Indonesia Independence Day".

As we all know that every August 17, the Indonesian nation celebrates its independence day. Especially by holding a flag ceremony to raise the Red and White saka and honor the services of the heroes who fought to expel the invaders. So, we can enjoy independence as it is now.

But, the sense of independence is interpreted differently by today's generation, which is only interpreted every August 17. This argument is felt to be inappropriate because independence itself is not only during the commemoration of RI's independence day, it remains faithful to our day then together in this Beloved Indonesia.

Therefore, not only on August 17, we talk about the spirit of unity, defend the country and appreciate the services of the heroes. However, every day our attitude and the words we say must reflect all that. If we are filled with a great sense of love for our homeland, then we can defend this country with all its honor and no other nation will dare to invade us again.

So many sentences that I can convey on this vibrant morning, hopefully can be useful and embedded in the hearts of all of us, especially the younger generation. If there are words wrong, I apologize.

Wassalamau'alaikum Wr. WB.

5. teks pidato bahasa Inggris tentang kemerdekaan dan terjemahannya​


Good morning, my respected teachers, my beloved friends, and ladies and gentlemen who present here. I feel so honored to be standing here in front of all of you on this special occasion. First of all, let us extend our gratitude toward the Almighty whose blessing and grace are always with us so that we all can be here on this sunny morning in good condition.

For decades, our country has lived in freedom. This freedom was not easy to obtain. Sweat, blood, and tears had been sacrificed by our heroes to achieve freedom. For decades, we have celebrated Independence Day with happiness and honor. For decades, we also have built this country in hope and despair.

Ladies and gentlemen, the Independence Day celebration is the right moment to look back at the past of how our heroes were struggling to gain freedom. Learning the history of our country is one way to make this nation big. History can make us get to know Indonesia better and love it even more. Looking back at the past can also help us to think about what we should do to maintain freedom and to make this nation become a better country to live in.

Although our nation has tasted freedom for a very long time, this country has still many aspects and situations to improve. It is sad to see that there are still many civilians living in poverty, corruption is everywhere, and the economy seems to get worst day by day. It looks like we still have a long way to go to become one big fabulous nation. Even so, that is not impossible to achieve. With the natural and human resources we have, I believe we can pursue our dream to become the greatest country.

Respected teachers and dearest friends, freedom is something we should grateful for. To show our gratitude, we should contribute positively to society. We don’t have to be the president or the ministry first to contribute to our country. Even a small act, like being respectful and tolerant towards others, plays a really important part in developing this country. Every person has a role to help to make our nation better and always peaceful. For example, students’ roles are to study hard, and teachers’ roles are to educate students. Of course, we ideally have to do our roles right so we can obtain our goals faster.

Ladies and gentlemen, to end my speech, I would like to ask you to pray to God for us and this country. Let’s ask Him to help us maintaining peacefulness so that our children and grandchildren will also be able to celebrate Independence Day with the freedom right in their hearts.

9. tolong contoin pidato bahasa inggris tema kemerdekaan


Good morning and best wishes for u!

The honorable ( nama kepala sekolah dan nama sekolah )

The honorable Mr. Mrs. Teachers and Staff
And my beloved friend

Ladies and Gentlemen,

Today we are celebrating our country’s independence day. Did you know that 70 years ago people before us fought by sacrificing their lives and property to repel invaders from our land. They fought very bravely in taking away and maintaining the independence of our country from the colonialist’s arms and in the middle of rain of bullets that can shoot them anytime. All that they had done is for getting the independence of our nation. Thanks to them so that we all can feel the freedom until today.

Ladies and Gentlemen,

The independence that we can fell at this time does not mean that we have finished the struggle. Instead at this point we have to fight to defend the independence of Indonesia. We as Indonesian people, especially the young generation has a very important role for the progress and peace of our country because the young generation is spearheading of our nation’s struggle today. However, in reality, the young generation now mostly being individualism, being hedonism, and has low nationalism spirit. Surely this is a huge problem for our nation, what will happen to our nation, if the young generation is already lost values of noble character and spirit to live and work together and help each other.

Ladies and Gentlemen,

The spirit of nationalism in the young generation is very important. The spirit of nationalism can establish awareness and loyalty to the nation and the country regardless of their ethnicity, race and religion. By having the spirit of nationalism we can establish harmony among the people. That is why we must not remain silent with what happened to the young generation of today. Therefore, the spirit of nationalism should we inject into the souls of our young generation. By raising the encouragement to them and giving them motivation to young. This is becoming important because the young generation is the next generation who will be cantilever of Indonesian nation.

Ladies and Gentlemen,

By the nationalism spirit, we as the young generation can continue the heroes ‘struggle who have served us well. If they had fought with weapons, we struggled with books and stationery to fight for Indonesian nation. We must always be active and always optimistic to provide the best for our beloved homeland. That is all we can achieve by learning, having achievement, scenting Indonesian name in the world and filling the independence of Indonesia as the best as you can do with high spirit of nationalism and patriotism.

Ladies and Gentlemen,

We must believe that we as a young generate will be successors are able to give change for this beloved nation. Good luck, work hard to be a better young generation for the future of our nation and our own future.

Ladies and Gentlemen,

I think what I say is enough. If there are mistakes in this short speech, I apologize. The last I say thank you for your attention.

Long live my Indonesia!

13. pidato bahasa inggris singkat tentang kemerdekaan beserta artinya


    Ladies and gentlemen, in commemorating our independence day today, we once again have to celebrate it in the pandemic Covid-19 situation. Even so, I ask all of you to keep fighting and never give up. Because our fight is against the pandemic is also a form of our appreciation and continuation of the struggle of our heroes.

    Our independent heroes fight with the colonial without rest, even with limited weapons. In this modern era, we are facing a different challenge, a different enemy. We are faced with an invisible enemy in a virus, one that is not fought with weapons, but with masks and vaccines.

    Therefore I ask the people of the audience to stay strict and always follow the health protocol by using masks, washing hands, keeping distance, and get vaccinated.

    As a closing, let's lower our heads for a moment to remember the heroes that have sacrificed for us. Our independent heroes as well as health heroes that had fought and is still fighting in this pandemic.

Terjemahan:

    Bapak dan ibu sekalian, pada hari peringatan kemerdekaan kita kali ini, sekali lagi kita masih berada di tengah pandemi Covid-19. Namun demikian saya mengajak Anda semua untuk terus berjuang dan tidak pernah menyerah. Karena perjuangan kita melawan pandemi ini adalah suatu wujud penghargaan dan melanjutkan perjuangan para pahlawan.

    Para pahlawan kemerdekaan berjuang melawan penjajah tanpa kenal lelah, walaupun bersenjatakan senjata seadanya. Di masa modern ini, kita menghadapi tantangan yang berbeda, musuh yang berbeda. Kita dihadapkan kepada musuh yang tidak terlihat berupa virus, yang bukan dilawan dengan senjata, melainkan dengan masker dan vaksin.

    Untuk itu saya mengajak hadirin sekalian untuk terus taat dan semangat dalam mengikuti protokol kesehatan dengan memakai masker, mencuci tangan, menjaga jarak, dan melakukan vaksinasi.

    Sebagai penutup, mari kita menundukkan kepala sejenak untuk mengenang jasa para pahlawan yang telah berkorban untuk kita. Baik pahlawan kemerdekaan, maupun pahlawan kesehatan yang telah dan tengah berjuang di masa pandemi ini.

Pembahasan

Pidato adalah kegiatan berbicara di depan umum atau orasi untuk menyampaikan suatu pendapat, dan umumnya memiliki tema khusus.

Teori pidato Bahasa Inggris sama saja dengan pidato Bahasa Indonesia. Dalam membuat pidato sebaiknya menggunakan kalimat yang mudah dimengerti agar pesan kita tersampaikan dengan baik kepada para pendengar.

Dalam membuat pidato berbahasa Inggris, mungkin akan lebih mudah apabila kamu membuatnya dulu dalam Bahasa Indonesia, baru kemudian menerjemahkannya ke dalam Bahasa Inggris.
