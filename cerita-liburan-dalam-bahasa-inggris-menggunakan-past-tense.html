Last holiday I went to Yogyakarta. I went there by a plane with my mom, dad, two sisters, and my great-grandmother. It took almost two hours to arrive at Yogyakarta. When I arrived there, my grandparents picked us at the airport. They drove a car by themselves and my dad put all the luggages in the bagage. We had lunch together in our favorite's restaurant called Ayam Marhein. It wasn't an authentic food from Yogya, but we all liked it so much. After that, we went to my grandparents' house. We packed out all of our stuffs and took a rest. We stayed in Yogya for three days two nights. We didn't stay for too long since we brought my great-grandmother. The next day, we went to Borobudur Temple. Me and my sisters were going to the top of the temple, but my mom, dad, my grandparents, and my great-grandmother stayed in the car. It was very hot and we decided to leave the temple. We had gudeg for our lunch. At night, my grandparents asked me and my sisters to stroll around the city just want to show the city at night. We were so excited and stolled around by a car. We went to Alun-Alun Yogyakarta to see people ride a bike, eat, and have fun there. Then, we passed the Tugu Yogyakarta. After that, we went home. On the third day, me and my family were going to Malioboro to buy some souvenirs and had lunch at Raminten before we went back to Jakarta. The last day was quite hectic because we had to arrive at the airport at 14.00. Fortunately, everything went based on the plan. We said goodbye to our grandparents and we flew back to Jakarta at 15.00. It was a short trip, but I really enjoyed my holiday with my family.
