Cats


Cats also called the domestic cat or house cat (with its scientific name: Felis silvestris catus or Felis catus) is a type of carnivorous mammal of the family Felidae. The word "cat" generally refers to a "cat" that has been tamed, but can also refer to the "big cats" such as lions and tigers.


Cats are considered as "perfect carnivore" with teeth and particular digestive tract. The first premolar and molar teeth form a pair of fangs on each side of the mouth that works effectively as a pair of scissors to tear the meat. Although these features also exist in the Canidae or dog, but these traits are better developed in cats. Unlike other carnivores, cats eat almost non vegetable substance. Bears and dogs sometimes eat berries, roots, or honey as a supplement, while cats only eat meat, usually freshly killed prey. In captivity, cats can not adapt to a vegetarian diet because they can not synthesize all the amino acids they need from plant material; it is in contrast with domesticated dogs, which commonly are fed a mixture of meat and vegetables and sometimes it can adapt to a completely vegetarian meal.


Cats have mingled with human life since at least 6000 BC, from the skeleton of the cat found on the island of Cyprus. The ancient Egyptians of 3500 BC have used cats to keep away the rats or other rodents from the barn where the crops were saved.Currently, the cat is one of the most popular pet in the world. Cats that his lines are recorded officially as a cat breeds or pure breed are Persian, Siamese, Manx, and the sphinx. These kinds of cat are usually bred in official captivity animal. The number of purebred cat is only 1% of all cats in the world; the rest is a cat with mixed ancestry such as wild cats or domestic cats.

6. buatlah recount text tentang Bangun tidur, Mandi, Gosok gigi, makai seragam Makai sepatu ,minta uang saku ,Datang terlambat. teks recountnya harus mengikuti struktur teks, yaitu : orientation, events ( peristiwa ), dan re orientation.​


Recount text about morning activity:

Orientation:

Yesterday  I woke up late because last night I slept very late. In the morning I woke up at 6 am, then rushed to take a shower and brush my teeth. I remember that day there was a flag ceremony at the school. I put on my uniform and shoes in a hurry, I didn't even have time to eat breakfast.

Events:

I ran to school for fear of missing the flag ceremony. The flag ceremony started at 7 o'clock. But school was still far away and I was already very tired of running.

Re-orientation:

Finally I arrived at school at 7.15, I was 15 minutes late. I received the punishment of sweeping the school's front yard along with other people who were also late. But I was relieved because that day I still went to school.

Pembahasan

Recount text seperti di atas digunakan untuk menyampaikan pengalaman pribadi yang ditulis dengan menggunakan format past tense. Recount text seperti juga narrative text dibuat dengan struktur berikut.

Orientation yang berisi pengenalan latar belakang kejadian. Events yang berisi berbagai kejadian yang dialami penulis. Re-orientation yang berisi penutup atau kesimpulan. Pelajari lebih lanjut

b. makalah eksposisiMakalah

Makalah adalah salah satu produk cara membuat karya tulis ilmiah yang memuat kajian tentang suatu masalah di lingkungan sekitar. Landasan pembahasanya adalah keberadaan data di lapangan yang bersifat empiris-objektif. Kajian yang termuat dalam makalah menggunakan pola pikir yang deduktif dan induktif. Pola pikir deduktif adalah cara berpikir yang ditangkap atau diambil dari pernyataan yang bersifat umum lalu ditarik kesimpulan yang bersifat khusus. Sedangkan pola pikir induktif adalah cara mempelajari sesuatu yang bertolak dari hal-hal atau peristiwa khusus untuk menentukan hukum yang umum.

Makalah juga bisa diartikan sebagai karya akademis produk dari cara membuat karya tulis ilmiah yang diterbitkan pada suatu jurnal yang bersifat ilmiah. Salah satu karya ilmiah ini juga biasanya digunakan sebagai persyaratan ujian pada suatu mata kuliah. Terlebih lagi, dalam tugas tersebut biasanya mahasiswa dituntut untuk memuat saran pemecahan tentang suatu secara ilmiah kedalam makalah mereka. Walau makalah adalah bentuk paling sederhana diantara karya tulis ilmiah lainnya, bahasa yang digunakan dalam makalah tetaplah bahasa yang tegas dan lugas.



13. tolong buatkan recount text liburan memmakai verb 2


Contoh Recount text:

We went to Medan last year to visit our family. It was a school holiday. I went there with my little sister. Our grandmother lives in a big old house with many rooms. She has many chickens in the backyard. When we were there, I fed the chicken in the morning. My sister and I walked to a nearby river and play there for hours. We helped our grandmother taking care of the plants and the chickens.

One Saturday morning, we walked to the local market to buy some fresh vegetables and fish. I was so excited, we also bought some traditional toys that I can play with my sister. We stayed for two weeks at my grandmother's house. It was a very memorable school holiday.

Pembahasan

Recount text adalah teks yang ditulis untuk menceritakan kembali kejadian atau peristiwa yang terjadi pada masa lampau.

Tujuan dari recount text adalah untuk memberikan informasi atau menghibur pembacanya. Dalam menulis recount text, tenses yang digunakan adalah

SIMPLE PAST TENSE

Simple Past Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menjelaskan aktivitas yang dilakukan pada waktu lampau dan sudah selesai. Bentuk umum dari Simple Past Tense adalah:

(+) S + Verb 2

(-) S + did not + infinitive without to

(?) Did + S + infinitive without to

PAST CONTINUOUS TENSE

Bentuk umum dari Past Continuous tense adalah

S + was/were + verb-ing

contoh kalimat yang menggunakan tenses ini adalah:

I was cooking in the kitchen when the postman came.We were watching TV at 8 PM last night

Verb 2 atau past tense dapat dibagi 2 menurut perubahannya, yaitu:

Regular Verb, yaitu jika perubahannya teratur dengan menambahkan -d/-ed pada akhiran kata kerja, contohnya:

walk - walkedcook - cookedhelp - helpedstudy - studied

Irregular Verb, yaitu jika perubahannya tidak teratur, contohnya:

teach - taughtsee - sawwrite - wroteeat -ate
