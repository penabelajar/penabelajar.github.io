Penjelasan:

Simple past tense

*She was here yeasterday (Dia berada disini kemarin)

*They were happy last week (Mereka gembira minggu lalu)

*He was with me yeasterday (Dia bersamaku kemarin?

Simple present tense

*They are at school every day (Mereka berada di sekolah setiap hari)

*She is here every morning (Dia berada disini setiap pagi)

*We are go to school every day (Kami berangkat ke sekolah tiap hari)

*She will go to Bandung tomorrow (Dia akan berangkat ke Bandung besok)

13. Contoh dialog Simple present tense, dan simple present continues tense


CREATE A  DIALOG BY USING SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE AND PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE !

__________________________

JAWABAN ;

( in the school backyard )

Dee       : Zoe, our friends are arguing Gea's daily activity ! Do you know her?

Zoe       : Certainly, she is my neighbor. She is a good girl. She often helps her friends who need help. She helps her mother everyday. She usually comes early to school. She always sits on the first row seat . She is such a diligent girl. She loves reading any kind of books.

Dee       :  By the way , what is she doing now during the  school break?

Zoe       : She is reading some books in the school library right now. Do you want to speak to her? I will introduce you later.

Dee       ;  No, actually, i would like to lend a novel.

Zoe       ; okay, i can take you to her house after school. Isn't it the correct time ?

Dee      : Yes. Thank you for your kindness, zoe.

PENJELASAN :

SIMPLE PRESENT TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS TENTANG FAKTA KEBENARAN ( GENERAL TRUTH ) , FENOMENA

=> KEBIASAAN YANG DILAKUKAN BERULAN-ULANG ( HABITUAL ACITIVITY/ DAILY ACTIVITY )

=> MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN FREKUENSI =

ALWAYS, USUALLY, EVERYDAY, SELDOM, RARELY, OFTEN, TWICE A WEEK, ONCE A DAY

=> POLA/RUMUS

SUBJECT + VERB ( S/ES ) + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT  ( VERBAL SENTENCE )

SUBJECT +TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + NOUN/ADJECTIVE/ADVERB  ( NOMINAL )

PRESENT CONTINUOUS TENSE

=> MEMBAHAS KEGIATAN YANG DILAKUKAN KETIKA SEDANG DIBICARAKAN SESAAT.

=>MENGGUNAKAN KETERANGAN WAKTU =

RIGHT NOW, NOW, STILL, AT THE MOMENT

=>POLA / RUMUS =

SUBJECT+TOBE(IS/AM/ARE) + VERB-ING + OBJECT/COMPLEMENT
