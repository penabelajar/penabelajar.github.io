Penjelasan:

CAUSE AND EFFECT (CAUSATIVE) adalah hubungan antara dua hal atau action, dimana hal yang satu menyebabkan terjadinya hal yang lain. Untuk dapat menentukan 'cause' dari suatu kalimat yaitu dengan mempertanyakan mengapa hal itu terjadi. Untuk lebih jelasnya:

CAUSE: The reason for something happening

EFFECT: What happened

CAUSE& EFFECT: explain why something happens

Untuk menghubungkan antara cause dan effect digunakan signal words. Beberapa signal words yang sering digunakan dalam cause and effect adalah:

due toowing tobecausebecause ofsincesoastherefore

Signal words due to, because of, thanks to dan owing to diikuti oleh noun

Signal words seperti because, since, so, as, therefore diikuti oleh verb

Berikut contoh kalimat cause and effect:

The sun was hot, so my ice cream melted.Dani is absence today because of his sickness.Dani is absence today because he is sickI didn't have breakfast this morning, so I feel so hungry nowSince I don't feel well, I stay in bed today.Pelajari Lebih Lanjut

2. Siti has planned a trip to her uncle's house because she loves her cousins.

Cause : Siti loves her cousins

Effect : she planned a trip to her uncle's house

3. The green house gases trap the heat in the air, so the earth becomes warmer .

Cause : the heat is trapped  in the air

Effect : the Earth becomes warmer

4. Because the Sumatran tigers were almost extinct, the Indonesian government declared them as endangered species.

Cause : the Sumatran tigers were almost extinct

Effect : It was declared as endangered species

5. Animals are becoming extinct because humans are moving into their habitats.

Cause : Humans are moving into animals' habitats

Effect : Animals are becoming extinct

Pembahasan

Cause (sebab) merupakan penyebab mengapa sesuatu dapat terjadi.

Effect (akibat) adalah hasil dari apa yang telah terjadi.

Cause dan Effect sangat berkaitan satu sama lain. "If there is an effect, there must be a cause. If there is a cause, that could lead to effects." Artinya, jika ada akibat, pasti ada penyebab. Jika ada penyebab, dapat menimbulkan akibat.

Dalam membuat kalimat cause and effect, diperlukan conjunction (kata hubung) seperti: because, since, as, so, for, therefore.

Contoh :

He was angry because we were late.It is raining heavily outside, so I can't come to your party.Since you can speak English, you have many friends from other countries.Tasya can't go to school today for she is unwell.The boss was happy as John did the work very well.The school is under construction, therefore I don't go to school today.
