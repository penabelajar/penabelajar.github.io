2. apa imaji visual,taktil lagu maher zein ingsa allah


Answer: 

The song entitled 'Insha Allah' by Maher Zein is one of the most phenomenal Islamic pop song which has been known worldwide and has been made into several other foreign languages. To see the visual imagery and meaning which can be felt by our senses, you need to see the lyric as well as the music video to get the actual tone of the song. 
- The visual imagery is the words that can give the image to your sight. Such lies on 'all you see is night and darkness all round, you feel so helpless you can't see which way to go.' Those words aren't clearly giving the image as it's just regular common words. Yet, such is well-described in the music video about how people who are about to commit sinful mistake, realise that such is forbidden and then you see the light in Allah, the Almighty God, to repent. 
 - the meaning felt by our sense is as a matter of fact, not in the song as the words used are common ones, yet you can suddenly feel that you're weeping as the tears goes down on your cheek when you really listen to the song.

13. Lagu Maher Zein, insya Allah Bahasa inggris, cari liriknya yang ada kalimat adjective, Subject, dan apa pesan dari lagu itu? Catatan : Jawab Pakai Bahasa Inggris


Answer: 

In this popular pop religious song by Swedish artist, Maher Zein , some of the adjective sentences (the sentence which has adjectives in it) are
- You're so confused
- It's way too late
- He's never far away
- you're so alone

Meanwhile, the subject of this song is us the listener and all Moslem in the world who are familiar with it. This song is more like an encouragement for us to have faith in Allah, no matter what. 
The message in this song is that we should keep praying and believing in Allah's blessing despite the misfortune, or feeling despair as Allah the Almighty God would listen your prayers and grant your wish whenever it's considered proper in time.

14. Perbandingan umur Zein dan Lukas 3:2. Jika jumlah umur 30 tahun maka hitunglah umur Zein dan Lukas!


Jawab:

Zein= 3/(3+2)x30 tahun

= 3/5 x 30

= 3x6

= 18 tahun

Lucas = 2/(3+2)x 30 tahun

= 2/5 x 30

= 2x6

= 12 tahun
