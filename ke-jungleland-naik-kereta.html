Recreation is usually known by the name of The Jungle which is one of the tourist attractions in london and you can make as a destination for family recreation as well as a tourist attraction education for school children who want to hold a sports event while learning.
The Jungle Water Adventure was built in an area of ​​Housing Bogor Nirwana Residence (BNR) with a background of Mount Salak. The scenery here is very beautiful and the air was too cool, standing on an area of ​​3 hectares with about 60% of the total area is an open green area located in Bogor south.
The Jungle Bogor Nirwana Water Adventure
The Jungle Bogor Nirwana Water Adventure
Location The Jungle Water Adventure in Housing Bogor Nirwana Residence (BNR) is located on Jl. Dreded Heroes, City of South Bogor - West Java, Java, Indonesia. Location of housing Bogor Nirwana Residence can be reached easily with a travel time of about 10 minutes drive from Bogor toll gate.
Nirwana Epicentrum is the name for an integrated commercial area located within the housing Bogor Nirwana Residence and The Jungle is a first amusement rides occupy the commercial district. In this Epicentrum Nirwana will also be equipped with various facilities such as the International School, University, Orchard Walk Mall, Orchard Walk Arcade, Aston Hotels & Condotel, Flona Walk, Golf 18 holes and Lifestyle Center.
The Jungle Water Adventure offers a wide variety of rides games such as:
kiddie Pool
Kiddie Pool - Represents a pool for the kids but even adults also love it, as if they want to repeat back to his childhood. The pool has two large pools, namely:
First pool with a water depth of about 20 cm to about 50 cm, containing a variety of water games such as dolphins toys, mini slider, seesaw and also various fountains which is continuously spouted.
Both pools containing a variety of games such as water cannons, water bucket and rope tight.
Slide Pool - Here there is a slide / Launcher which is a game for children and adults, here visitors can skate using a rotating tire belt of the winding of heights and ended up in the pool.
Racer Slide - Has four lanes with a height of about 8 meters, suitable for visitors who like a challenge and want to test the guts.
Spiral Slide and Tube Slide - These rides are 2 kinds of games are a mainstay of The Jungle. Spiral Slide is a launcher that looks open water. While Tube Slide is a water launcher that looks covered with a diameter of about 1 meter. Each slide has a height of 12 meters with a length of about 100 meters or less, visitors can skate with tires and can compete with friends or other visitors and will end up in the pool.
Leisure Pool - It is a place to relax at the center of the pool and can be used by visitors to eat or drink. Facilities available at the Leisure Pool is like a fountain area for futsal, a lazy river pool slide, 4D cinema room, facilities for rinse water in the bathroom and foodcourt.
Area Fountain Futsal
Area Fountain Futsal - futsal fountain In this area there is an outdoor stage is large enough to be used for a wide variety of entertainment, such as bands playing music and singing to entertain the visitors.
For those of you who want to play futsal, here also been provided futsal arena. The uniqueness futsal arena futsalnya here is equipped with multiple points of fountains that exist on the surface so that when the playing field will be interspersed with a spray of water that comes from below and is quite hard. His goal was also very unique, the material is not made of iron, but made from water that seeps straight up.
Cinema 4D - is a four-dimensional cinema first in the city of Bogor and the second in Indonesia after Dufan Ancol (Ancol Fantasy World). By using a special glasses, visitors can feel the sensation of its own during the watch in cinema 4D. When you're watching can feel a shock in a chair, a spray of water, the whirring thing that crosses between the legs until smelled something burning.
Ghost House - In Ghost House there are various hallways and also some mechanical doll that is ready to scare the visitors, so it will create an atmosphere of horror.
Foodcourt - Here there is a wide variety of foods derived from menu to menu of western Indonesia. While enjoying a meal there you can also enjoy the scenic beauty of the mountain bark.
