SIMPLE PAST TENSE

(Suatu aktivitas yang telah terjadi di masa lampau)

Rules

→ (+)S + Verb2 + O/C

(-) S + did + not + Verb1 + o/c

(?) Did + S + Verb1 + o/c ?

Yes, S + did

No, S + did + not + Verb1
