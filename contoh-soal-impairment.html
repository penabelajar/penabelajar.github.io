10. An impairment of language function due to localized brain damage that leads to difficulty in understanding and/or producing language is ....


Jawaban:

Aphasia is a language disorder that is caused by an injury to specific parts of the brain that control language. The injury can occur because of a stroke, traumatic brain injury, or brain infection (encephalitis). The three kinds of aphasia are Broca's aphasia, Wernicke's aphasia, and global aphasia

Penjelasan:

Aphasia is caused by damage to one or more of the language areas of the brain

Aphasia is a disorder caused by damage to areas of the brain that produce and process language. As a result, people with this disorder have difficulty speaking, reading, writing and understanding language, so they cannot communicate well.

Indonesian :
Afasia adalah gangguan yang disebabkan oleh kerusakan pada area otak yang memproduksi dan memproses bahasa. Akibatnya, orang dengan gangguan ini mengalami kesulitan berbicara, membaca, menulis dan memahami bahasa, sehingga tidak bisa berkomunikasi dengan baik.
