– 2 leaves of spring onion, finely sliced
– 1 carrot, sliced ​​matchsticks
– 5 spring onions, mixed
– 3 cloves garlic, mixed
– 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce
– 1 tablespoon salt soy sauce
– salt as needed
– pepper as needed
– 1 teaspoon of chicken powder
– 125 grams of sprouts
– vegetable oil as needed
– fried onions as needed for topping

How To Make Fried Noodles
– Fry two eggs, roll it, slice it finely, set aside.
– Heat 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil, add the other eggs, stir and set aside.
– Then heat 3 tablespoons of vegetable oil, add meatballs, stir it well.
– Then put onion and garlic, stir evenly, add scrambled egg, put seasoning ingredients such as soy sauce, pepper, salt, etc as you need.
– For the last, add the other ingredients such as carrots, chicken powder, sprouts, and stir it evenly, then set aside.
– The fried noodles is ready to serve.

7. cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa inggris?


ingredients

Noodles
Onions
Garlic
Olive oil
Tomatoes
Lemoncarrots

Steps

1. Prepare all the necessary ingredients on the table or another flat surface. Make sure you have everything you need. Chop the onions, garlic, carrots, and tomatoes on the cutting board. Cut the ingredients into very small cube-like pieces so the noodles will get the taste of every ingredient.


2. Heat your frying pan and add some olive oil in it while it's heating up.


3. Open the noodles from the package and crumble them into average pieces (not too small though). Smaller pieces have a higher chance of cooking all the way through.


4. Your crumbled noodles should look a bit small like you see in the picture.


5. After your frying pan is hot enough with the oil and such, just put all of the chopped ingredients into the frying pan.Leave it there for a while and stir.


6. If the chopped ingredients are ready after some time, add the crumbled noodles there. Stir it for a while.


7. If the noodles smell great with the others, add the seasoning that came with the packet. If you didn't get one, then just add a pinch a salt or more according to your taste. Stir it up again. Now add some water in it. The noodle shouldn't sink in the water. Only 75 % of it should be.


8. Keep out creation in the frying pan until all the water soaks up in the noodles.Now, squeeze about half a lime or according to your taste. There you have it! You're now ready to serve the president some nice fried noodles.

12. Cara membuat mie goreng dalam bahasa Inggris sedikit bahannya.


Jawaban:

Ingredients :

1 pack of egg noodle750 ml water7 tbsp sweet soy sauce4 tbsp olive oil or regular oil6 cloves garlic12 piece chili or as you like2 tsp salt3 eggs2 tbsp white ground pepper1 cube maggi or knorr (chicken stock powder)1 pack bok choy1 small red onion6 piece candlenut4 stalks scallion slice it as you like

*tsp = tea spoon & tbsp = table spoon

How to make :

Prepare a pan with 750 ml water and boil the egg noodle for about 2 or 3 minutes add olive oil and stir it (so the noodle is not sticky) lift and set asideBlend the seasoning : onion, garlic, chili, and candlenut Scramble the eggs with a little salt and set asideAfter the noodle cool enough take the noodle and add sweet soy sauce then stir it until the noodles had a perfect brown color Prepare a pan with 3 tbsp olive oil, add the blend seasoning stir it until it had a very good smell and add bok choy (if you like add beef, shrimp, chicken, or sausage add at this time) then stir it until bok choy half cooked add the noodle and scramble egg then keep stir it well.Add salt, maggi (chicken stock powder), and white ground pepper then mix it wellTaste it and correct the taste then add scallion mix it wellServe the fried noodle with some shrimp crackers or fish crackers.

Enjoy your Simple Fried Noodle ! nom nom nom

14. cara memasak mie goreng untuk pemula​


1. Sediakan wajan, sendok, dan mie instan yang mau dimasak

2. Letakkan wajan berisi air secukupnya di atas kompor

3. Nyalakan kompor, dan tunggu air mendidih lalu masukkan mie instan

4. Sementara menunggu mienya mendidih, tuang semua bumbu ke wadah

5. Jika mie sudah mendidih, aduk menggunakan sendok lalu matikan kompor dan tiriskan airnya menggunakan saringan

6. Setelah sudah tidak ada airnya lagi, tuang ke dalam wadah yang telah berisi bumbu yang sudah dituang tadi

7. Aduk hingga merata dengan sendok.

8. Makan, kalau abis jangan lupa untuk cuci piring nya:)


15. cara memasak mie goreng spesial dengan telur​


Jawaban:

BAHAN-BAHAN

1 bungkus Mie Telor

Sawi Hijau

Bakso / Sosis (Aku pake sosis yaa)

1 butir Telur

Daun Bawang

3 siung Bawang Merah

2 siung Bawang Putih

1/2 sdt Lada

1 sdt Ketumbar

2 butir Kemiri

Kecap

LANGKAH

1.Pertama rebus mie telor selama kurang lebih 3-5 menit, supaya tdk lengket / mie ny menggumpal tambahkan sedikit minyak ya Jika sudah selesai tiriskan

2.Kemudian haluskan bumbu (bawang merah, bawang putih, lada, ketumbar, kemiri)

Tumis bumbu yg sudah dihaluskan, beri sedikit air supaya bumbu tdk gosong

3.Masukkan telur sambil di orak-arik, tambahkan bakso / sosis, daun bawang & sawi aduk terus sampai sawi nya layu jangan lupa tambahkan garam

4.Dan yg terakhir masukkan mie telur yg sudah direbus tadi aduk rata, tambahkan kecap secukupnya.

Jawaban:

bahan: mie instant, telur, air secukupnya

alat: wajan, piring, sendok, garpu

cara membuat:

1.siapkan wajan

2.masukan air secukupnya

3.nyalakan kompor, tunggu sampai mendidih

4.selagi menunggu air mendidih, buka bungkus mie instant, masukan semua bumbu nya kedalam piring

5.setelah air mendidih masukan mie, dan pecahkan telur diatasnya

6.tunggu sampai matang

7.setelah matang, angkat dan tiriskan mie lalu masukan kedalam piring sambil aduk sampai bumbunya tercampur rata

8.taruh telur di atasnya

9.siapkan sendok dan garpu untuk alat makan

10.Dan mie siap disajikan
