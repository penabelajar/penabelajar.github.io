2. buatlah cerita masa kecil di taman kanak kanak, jangan terlalu panjang dan jangan terlalu pendek minta tolong bsok dikumpulin


When I was little, my family often moved to various regions in Indonesia. We usually moved every two years as my father was assigned to head one of the branch offices where he worked, which were located throughout Indonesia.

My two siblings and I had to change schools frequently and adapt to the culture and local language of the new place to be able to communicate and make friends.

Saturdays and Sundays were my favorite days because my parents usually took us to visit touristic places and restaurants that served local specialties. My favorite activity was collecting handicrafts, which were typically made of skins, shells or bamboos.

When I was little, there were no computer or games as they are now, so I usually spent a lot of time playing outdoors with friends who lived near my house. We often played together after school and had lunch.

My favorite games and activities included badminton, jump rope, hopscotch, running, hide and seek, baseball, riding bikes, or picking mangoes from the trees in front yards, walking at the paddy dike while singing, chatting, and flying kites.

A beautiful childhood is difficult to forget.

Cerpen Karangan: Dinda Amaria Purba

Kategori: Cerpen Anak, Cerpen Pengalaman Pribadi

Lolos moderasi pada: 9 August 2015

Hai teman-teman, aku mau cerita nih tentang masa kecilku. Kalau dipikir-pikir yang enak didengar itu masa lalu, terutama masa laluku. Sebenarnya sih banyak yah masa laluku, tapi aku mau nyeritain beberapa saja.

Salah satu cerita menarik menurutku adalah waktu umurku 5 tahun, tepatnya, aku masih anak TK nol kecil. Pada saat itu aku baru memasuki taman kanak-kanak, jadi aku belum kenal betul sama teman-temanku.

Singkat cerita, aku dikenal sebagai anak yang pendiam di TK tapi kalau di rumah enggak, nah jadi wajar saja kalau waktu itu aku belum mempunyai teman, aku juga merupakan anak yang susah bergaul dan aku juga tidak mempunyai nyali untuk berkenalan (gak tahu kenapa).Pada saat bel istirahat berbunyi aku tidak mempunyai teman untuk bermain, jadi aku sendirian aja deh mainnya. Aku bermain di bawah pohon, nah waktu ada batang pohon yang menurut aku kuat, entah kenapa aku jadi ingin bergelantungan di batang pohon itu. Saat aku bergelantungan tiba-tiba batang pohonnya patah terus aku ikut jatuh juga deh, rasanya sakit.Saat aku terjatuh ke bawah, dengan perasaan kaget dan malu ternyata ada yang menertawakanku karena aku malu aku cuma senyum aja. Waktu itu dia duluan yang kenalan sama aku tahu bahwa namanya adalah Gaby.

Seingatku Gaby itu kulitnya putih, rambutnya pendek terus badannya sedikit gemuk tapi paras wajahnya cantik. Setelah kejadian itu aku dan Gaby berteman akrab, kenapa? Karena istirahat kami selalu bersama, makan bekal juga selalu bersama, kadang sampai tukaran makanan.

Nah, kejadian itu tidak akan pernah kulupakan, karena walaupun aku jatuh dari batang pohon, walau itu rasanya cukup sakit. Aku dapat mempunyai teman baru yang akrab denganku Gaby, kalau nama lengkapnya sih aku gak tahu. Setelah aku lulus dari Taman Kanak-Kanak, sampai sekarang aku tidak pernah bertemu dengan Gaby.

Short Story: Dinda Amaria Purba

Category: Short Story Children, Short Story Personal Experience

Passed moderation on: 9 August 2015

Hi friends, I want to tell you about my childhood. If you think about it, what is pleasant to hear is the past, especially my past. Actually there are a lot of my past, but I want to tell a few.

One interesting story in my opinion is that when I was 5 years old, to be exact, I was still a small zero kindergarten child. At that time I just entered kindergarten, so I didn't really know my friends.

Long story short, I was known as a quiet child in kindergarten but if at home it wasn't, so it was only natural that at that time I didn't have friends yet, I was also a child who was difficult to get along with and I didn't have the guts to get acquainted (don't know why).

When the break bell rang, I didn't have friends to play, so I was alone playing it. I play under a tree, now when there is a tree trunk that I think is strong, I don't know why I want to hang on the tree trunk. When I suddenly hung, the tree trunk was broken and I fell too, it hurt.

When I fell down, feeling shocked and embarrassed there was someone who laughed at me because I was embarrassed that I just smiled. At that time he was the first acquaintance I knew that his name was Gaby.

As I recall, Gaby was white, her hair was short and her body was a little fat, but her face was pretty. After the incident, Gaby and I were friends, why? Because our breaks are always together, eating lunch is also always together, sometimes until food is exchanged.

Well, the incident will never be forgotten, because even though I fell from the trunk of a tree, even though it felt quite sick. I can have a new friend who is familiar with me Gaby, if I don't know the full name. After I graduated from kindergarten, until now I have never met Gaby

6. Buatlah cerita pendek menggunakan bahasa indonesia masa kecil sejak taman kanak-kanak sesudah itu terjemahkan ke dalan bahasa inggris​


Jawaban:

(bahasa Indonesia)

judul: Hujan Meteor yang Sebenarnya.

Peraturan permainan kami sebelum pulang ini sedikit berbeda. Pertama, kami membuat lingkaran dengan jarak antar pemain sekitar satu lencang kanan. Kemudian kami berputar di tempat sebanyak 10 putaran. Lumayan memusingkan untuk kami yang masih kelas 3 SD waktu itu.

Sepuluh putaran selesai dilakukan, permainan pun dimulai. Kepala yang masih pusing membuat beberepa temanku terjatuh dan jadi sasaran lemparan bola lumpur. Selanjutnya kami berlari meninggalkan sawah menuju arah yang berbeda. Bola lumpur tanpa henti dilempar dari depan ke belakang, begitu pula sebaliknya.

Hujan meteor lumpur terjadi sangat cepat hingga semua orang keluar dari sawah menuju jalan desa. Suara tawa menjadi akhir permainan kami sore itu. baju yang sudah full colour terkena lumpur menjadi oleh-oleh kami menuju rumah. Biasanya kami membersihkan lumpur di kolam warga yang banyak terdapat di desa.

(bahasa Inggris)

title: The Real Meteor Shower.

The rules of our game before going home are a little different. First, we make a circle with a distance between players about one right angle. Then we spin in place for 10 turns. Quite confusing for us who were still in 3rd grade at that time.

After ten rounds, the game begins. Heads that are still dizzy made some of my friends fall and become targets of throwing mud balls. Then we ran from the rice fields to a different direction. Mud balls were continuously tossed from front to back, and vice versa.

The mud meteor shower happened so fast that everyone came out of the fields towards the village road. Laughter ended our game that afternoon. clothes that were full color exposed to mud became our souvenirs to go home. Usually we clean the mud in the community pools that are widely available in the village.
