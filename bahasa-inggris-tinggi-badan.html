How to Increase Height

1. Exercise regularly

Exercise and regular physical activity are excellent for strengthening muscle and bone tissue, maintaining a healthy weight, and increasing growth hormone production.

2. Improve posture

The habit of standing, sitting, and sleeping with improper posture, such as frequent slouching, can make the body look shorter. On the other hand, with good and upright posture,The body will look taller and proportional.

3. Get enough rest

Because the process of growth and development of the body requires a high intake of energy, and one way to fill that energy is with adequate rest.

4. Consumption of balanced nutritious food

During the growth period, it is very important to meet the nutrients needed by the body, so that height growth can be maximized

15. Kuis [+50]: Terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa Inggris: - Tinggi badan Jiazheng adalah seratus delapan puluh tiga sentimeter. - Saya tinggi badannya, tapi rendah hatinya.


Jawaban:

Terjemahkan ke dalam bahasa Inggris:

- Tinggi badan Jiazheng adalah seratus delapan puluh tiga sentimeter.

= Jiazheng's height was one hundred and eighty-three centimeters.

- Saya tinggi badannya, tapi rendah hatinya.

= I am tall, but humble.

Semoga bisa membantu:)

Maaf jika ada yang salah

- Tinggi badan Jiazheng adalah seratus delapan puluh tiga sentimeter.

=Jiazheng's height is one hundred eighty three centimeters

- Saya tinggi badannya, tapi rendah hatinya.

= I'm tall, but humble.

Penjelasan:

Nomor 1

Tinggi badan=height

Jiazheng=Jiazheng

adalah=is

183=one hundred eighty three

sentimeter=centimeter

Nomor 2

Saya=I

tinggi=tall

tapi=but

rendah hati=humble
