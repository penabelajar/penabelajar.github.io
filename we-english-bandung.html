Jawaban:

1. (+) I study English. (-) I don't study English. (?) Do you study English? Yes, I do. OR No, I don't.

2. (+) They go to Bandung. (-)They don't go to Bandung. (?) Do they go to Bandung? Yes, I do. OR No, I don't.

3. (+) We put that book. (-) We don't put that book. (?) Do we put that book? Yes, I do. OR No, I don't

Penjelasan:

(+) S + V1

(-) S + DON'T / DOESN'T + V1

(?) DO / DOES + S + V1
