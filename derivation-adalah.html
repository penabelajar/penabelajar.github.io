1. contoh dari derivation adjective


1. Charming a Charm (verb) + Ing (suffix)He is so charming (Dia sangat menawan)

2. Childish a Child (noun) + ish (suffix)Marry is still childish (Marry masih kekanakan)

3. Useless a Use (verb) + less (suffix)This closet is useless (lemari ini tak berguna)

4. Talkative a Talk (verb) + ative (suffix)A teacher should be talkative (Seorang guru harus banyak bicara)

5. Ladylike a Lady (noun) + like (suffix)Mia is so ladylike (Mia sangat sopan)

6. Adventourous a Adventure (noun) +ous (suffix)Jacob is so adventurous (Jacob suka berpetualang)

7. Changable a Change (verb) +able (suffix)Can you make this format changable? ( bisakah kau mengubah format ini agar bisa diubah?)

8. Different a Differ (verb) + ent (suffix)I know I am different (Aku tahu aku berbeda)

9. Melted a Melt (verb) + ed(suffix)The ice cream is melted because of the high temperature in teh air (Es krimnya meleleh karena suhu yang tingi di udara)

2. perbedaan derivation dengan inflection?


Derivation : Perubahan penulisan kata karena berubah bentuk katanya, bisa dengan penambahan suffix atau prefix (Suffix itu akhiran dan prefix itu awalan)

Inflection : Perbuahan penulisan kata (dan juga cara bacanya) karena perbedaan jumlah, jenis kelamin, atau dalam jenis kalimat berbedaKATEGORI : DERIVATON + INFLEXION
TINGKAT : SMP/SMA/UMUM



THEIR DIFFERENCE ARE AS FOLLOW:
=> Derivation is usually associated with the formation of new words that are interconnected with other words. The formation of the word is usually done with the addition of affix so that the word formed will be different from the original form. This may happen because it may take the inflexional affixes of the new word class.


=> while inflection
usually produces a change of meaning greater than the original form of a form that has a rather idiosyncratic meaning because it
often changing the grammatical category of a root. in other words inflexon is some letters that added to nouns, verbs and adjectives in their different grammatical forms.

EXAMPLE
-----------------
DERIVATION
=> Speaker is derived from speak.✓
=> National is derived from nation.✓

INFLEXION
=> IF THERE ARE Two or more syllable words ending consonant-vowel-consonant that are stressed on the first syllable. We don't have to double the last consonant before the endings -ing/-ed/-er/-est.
Please pay attention to the examples below :
happen => happening ✓
visit => visited ✓

3. apa itu derivation? jelaskan dan sebutkan contohnya!


Derivation adalah suatu proses pembentukan kata melalui penambahan affix (imbuhan), yang dapat berupa prefix (awalan) dan atau suffix (akhiran). Kata baru yang dihasilkan akan memiliki arti yang berbeda dari kata dasarnya.Contoh Derivation:noun: threefold= threeroot + foldsuffixverb: enclose= enprefix + closerootadjective: interchangeable= interprefix + changeroot + ablesuffixadverb: retell= reprefix + tellroot

6. tolong artikan kedalam bahasa Indonesia D. grammar review: forming nouns form verbs.verbs can be formed as nouns by using derivation; its process is called verb-noun derivation. derivation is the process whereby the addition of affixes, chiefly prefixes and suffixes in english, to base forms results in the creation of New words.


D. tata bahasa ulasan: membentuk kata benda membentuk kata kerja.
kata kerja dapat dibentuk sebagai kata benda dengan menggunakan derivasi; Proses yang disebut derivasi verba-nomina. derivasi adalah proses dimana penambahan afiks, prefiks terutama dan sufiks dalam bahasa Inggris, untuk mendasarkan hasil bentuk dalam penciptaan kata baru.
