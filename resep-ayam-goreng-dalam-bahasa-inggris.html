6. resep nasi goreng bahasa inggris beserta artinya


Ingredients (Bahan bahan):1. 1 plate of White rice           (satu piring nasi putih)2. 2 Eggs                                 (2 telur)3. Tea spoon of Salt                (garam satu sendok teh)4. 2 cloves of Onions              (bawang putih 2 siung)5. 1 cloves of Garlic                (bawang merah 1 siung)6. 1/2 stick of Leeks                (setengah batang perai)7. Chili                                     (cabe)8. Pepper                                  (lada)9. Cooking oil                          (minyak goreng)10. Tomato sauce                     (saus tomat)11. complementary material    (bahan tambahan)

Tools (Peralatan)Frying pan (wajan)
Stove (kompor)
Spoon (sendok)
Spatula (Spatula)
Plate (Piring)

How to Make Fried Rice (Cara membuat nasi goreng):

1.   Prepare a frying pan and pour the cooking oil according to your taste(Persiapkan wajan dan tuangkan minyak makan berdasarkan seleramu)2.   Heat the frying pan by using a medium flame(Panaskan wajan dengan api sedang)3.   After the cooking oil is hot, put the garlic, onion and chili(Setelah minyak panas, masukan bawang merah, bawang putih dan cabe)4.   Stir all the materials until ripe and fragrant(Aduk semua bahan bahan sampai matang dan harum)5.   Put the supplementary materials according to your taste(Masukkan bahan bahan pokok sesuai selera anda)6.   Use Spatula to Stir until evenly cooked(Gunakan spatula untuk mengaduk hingga merata)7.   Put eggs and scramble until cooked(Masukkan telur dan aduk hingga termasak)8.   Put white rice and stir until mixed with all ingredients(Maukkan nasi putih dan aduk hingga tercampur dengan semua bahan bahan)9.   Add tomato sauce, pepper, and salt until all materials are blended(Tambahkan saus tomat, lada, dan garam sampai semua bahan bahan tercampur aduk)10. After everything on the frying pan was fragrant, serve into the plate.(Setelah semuan yang ada pada wajan matang, tiriskan pada piring)11. you could decorate with the sliced tomatoes and other complementary materials as you like to make your fried rice looks interesting.(Anda bisa mendekorasi dengan potongan tomat dan bahan tambahan lainnya sesuai dengan yang kalian suka untuk membuat nasi goreng anda terlihat menarik)12.  Your interesting fried rice is ready to eat (Nasi goreng anda yang menarik siap untuk dimakan).

8. identifikasikan resep nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris


Fried Rice is Indonesia traditional food. It is very popular food. It is easy to make it. It doesn't take too long time to make. The ingredients are easy to get.How to make Fried Rice? The following is the procedure text how to make fried rice.

Ingredients:

2 cloves of Garlic
2 onions cut into small pieces
1 table spoon of vegetable oil a plate of rice

.First, pound garlic and 2 pinches of salt.

.Second, heat vegetable oil on a frying pan.

.Third, put the pounded garlic and salt, and onions into the hot vegetable oil. Fry them a while.

.Fourth, put one plate of rice. Fry and mix them around 3 menit.

.Fifth, add two pinches of salt. Continue frying until the rice is hot enough.

.Finally, put the fried rice on a plate. Serve with a sunny-side up.

11. Resep nasi goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan artinya


Jawaban:

Material:

- 2 eggs

- 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

- 3 cherry tomatoes (cut to taste)

- 1 plate of rice

- 1 scallion (roughly chopped)

- Crackers (to taste)

- 1 tablespoon margarine

Ground spices:

- 2 cloves of garlic

- 5 pieces of cayenne pepper

- Sweet soy sauce (to taste)

- 3 cloves of shallot

- Salt to taste)

- Sugar (to taste)

How to cook:

a. Heat margarine until melted, cook scrambled eggs and set aside.

b. Add cooking oil and spices, stir well, cook until fragrant.

c. Enter rice into the egg mixture and seasoning, stir well. Add leeks, stir again.

d. Serve with cherry tomato topping and crackers as well as a little celery leaf garnish and red chili slices.

Artinya ;

Bahan:

- 2 telur

- 2 sendok makan minyak goreng

- 3 tomat ceri (potong secukupnya)

- 1 piring nasi

- 1 daun bawang (cincang kasar)

- Kerupuk (sesuai selera)

- 1 sendok makan margarin

Bumbu halus:

- 2 siung bawang putih

- 5 potong cabai rawit

- Kecap manis (secukupnya)

- 3 siung bawang merah

- Garam secukupnya)

- Gula untuk dicicip)

Cara memasak:

Sebuah. Panaskan margarin sampai meleleh, masak telur dadar dan sisihkan.

b. Tambahkan minyak goreng dan rempah-rempah, aduk rata, masak sampai harum.

c. Masukkan nasi ke dalam campuran telur dan bumbu, aduk rata. Tambahkan daun bawang, aduk lagi.

d. Sajikan dengan taburan tomat ceri dan kerupuk serta sedikit hiasan daun seledri dan irisan cabai merah.


12. Resep pisang goreng keju dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahannya


How to Make Fried Banana with Cheese

Ingredients:
- 4 bananas
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp of sugar
- 6 tbsp of flour
- water
- a pinch of salt
- cooking oil
- condensed milk
- grated cheese

Steps:
1. First, mix flour, egg, salt, and sugar in a mixing bowl. Mix it well.
2. Second, add water little by little. Whisk the batter vigorously until it is evenly smooth for coating. If the batter is too thin, you can add flour.
3. Next, peel the banana and cut it into two pieces.
4. After that, pour the cooking oil into the pan and let it heat.
5. Then, dip the banana into the batter to coat.
6. And then, place coated banana in a deep fryer and wait until it becomes golden and crispy.
7. Next, remove fried bananas from oil and drain it on paper napkins.
8. Finally, add condensed milk and grated cheese on the fried bananas.
9. Serve it while still warm.

TERJEMAHAN

Bahan:
- 4 buah pisang
- 1 butir telur
- 1 sendok teh gula
- 6 sendok makan tepung
- air
- sejumput garam
- minyak goreng
- susu kental
- keju parut

Langkah-langkah:
1. Pertama, campurkan tepung, telur, garam, dan gula ke dalam mangkok. Campur sampai rata.
2. Kedua, tambahkan air sedikit demi sedikit. Kocok adonan dengan kuat sampai halus untuk coating. Jika adonannya terlalu encer, kamu bisa menambahkan tepung.
3. Selanjutnya, kupas pisang dan potong menjadi dua bagian.
4. Setelah itu, tuang minyak goreng ke dalam panci dan biarkan minyaknya panas.
5. Lalu, masukkan pisang ke dalam adonan untuk dilapisi.
6. Dan kemudian, letakkan pisang yang sudah dilapisi ke dalam panci dan tunggu sampai berubah keemasan dan renyah.
7. Selanjutnya, angkat pisang goreng dari minyak dan tiriskan di atas serbet kertas.
8. Terakhir, tambahkan susu kental dan keju parut di atas pisang goreng sebagai taburan.
9. Sajikan selagi hangat.

13. Resep ikan goreng dalam bahasa inggris dan terjemahan nya


Ingredients: 1 tilapia

1 orange juice

1 tbsp vinegar

to taste cooking oil

Ground spices :

5 cloves of red onion

4 cloves garlic

1 tbsp coriander

2 1/2 segment turmeric

1 pecan fruit

1/2 segment of ginger

to taste salt

Step

Make sure the fish is clean from the scales, gills or the contents of the stomach, smooth on the outer edge.

Continue to coat the fish with orange juice and vinegar evenly, then set aside.

Prepare fine spices, wipe the fish and then sprinkle with fine spices, let stand for 30 minutes for the seasoning is completely absorbed

Prepare hot oil for frying, then fry the fish until golden or crisp.



Crispy and savory fried fish ready to serve.
