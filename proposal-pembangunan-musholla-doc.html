Skala denah = 1 : 250

Panjang musholla aebenarnya :

= 18 x 250 = 4.500 cm = 45 m

Lebar musholla aebenarnya :

= 14 x 250 = 3.500 cm = 35 m

Maka, luas musholla sebenarnya :

= 45 x 35 = 1.575 m²

Skala

= 1 : 250

= 1 cm : 250 cm

= 1 cm : 2,5 m

Luas musholla

= 18 × 2,5m × 14 × 2,5m

= 4½ × 10m × 3½ × 10m

= 9/2 × 7/2 × 100m²

= 63/4 × 100m²

= 1575m²
